# ISO/IEC 19896-3:2025 — Information security, cybersecurity and privacy protection — Requirements for the competence of IT security conformance assessment body personnel — Part 3: Knowledge and skills requirements for evaluators and reviewers according to the ISO/IEC 15408 series and ISO/IEC 18045

## L1: 基本資訊（必填）

**title**: Information security, cybersecurity and privacy protection — Requirements for the competence of IT security conformance assessment body personnel — Part 3: Knowledge and skills requirements for evaluators and reviewers according to the ISO/IEC 15408 series and ISO/IEC 18045

**original_content**: This document provides the specialized requirements for individuals to demonstrate competence in performing IT product security evaluations and reviews according to the ISO/IEC 15408 series and ISO/IEC 18045. NOTE: It is possible that evaluators and testers belong to bodies operating under ISO/IEC 17025 and reviewers belong to bodies operating under ISO/IEC 17065.

## L2: 責任轉移判定（必填）

**shift_type**: clarified
**enforcement_signal**: recommended
**rule_type**: Published
**affected_party**: Common Criteria evaluators, certification scheme operators, testing laboratories
**explanation**: 此為第 2 版標準，提供個人展現依據 ISO/IEC 15408 系列（Common Criteria）和 ISO/IEC 18045 執行 IT 產品安全評估和審查能力的專業要求。評估人員和測試人員可能隸屬於 ISO/IEC 17025 操作機構，審查人員可能隸屬於 ISO/IEC 17065 操作機構。46 頁的詳細內容反映了 Common Criteria 評估的複雜性。

## L3: 歷史脈絡（選填）

**replaces**: ISO/IEC 19896-3:2018 (standard/71122)
**context**: ISO/IEC 15408（Common Criteria）是全球最廣泛使用的 IT 產品安全評估標準，支援國際相互承認協議（CCRA）。本次第 2 版更新與 ISO/IEC 15408:2022 的重大改版同步，反映了評估方法學（CEM）的演進和新威脅環境的挑戰。
