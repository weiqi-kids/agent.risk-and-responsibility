# ISO/IEC 19798:2025 — Information technology — Office equipment — Method for the determination of toner cartridge yield for colour printers and multi-function devices that contain printer components

## L1: 基本資訊（必填）

**title**: Information technology — Office equipment — Method for the determination of toner cartridge yield for colour printers and multi-function devices that contain printer components
**iso_refno**: ISO/IEC 19798:2025
**edition**: 4
**date**: 2025-11-20
**ics_code**: 37.100.10
**technical_committee**: ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 28
**source_layer**: iso_standards
**original_content**: This document is limited to the evaluation of toner cartridge page yield for toner-containing cartridges (i.e. all-in-one toner cartridges and toner cartridges without a photoconductor) for colour electrophotographic print systems. This document can also be applied to the printer component of any multifunctional device that has a digital input printing path, including multi-function devices that contain electrophotographic printer components. This document is only intended for the measurement of toner cartridge page yield when printing on plain paper using cyan, magenta, yellow and black toner cartridges. No other claims can be made from this testing regarding quality, reliability, etc. This document is not for use with printers whose minimum printable size is equal to or greater than A3 or for photo-only printers. Application of this document for yield measurement of toner replenishment systems implies some procedural modifications specifically noted herein. This document is intended for equipment used in the office space and does not apply to production volume or large format printing machines where the major cost of ownership is not caused by the consumable yield measured in this document.

**shift_type**: clarified

**explanation**: 此為第 4 版標準，更新彩色電子攝影列印系統的碳粉匣頁數產出測量方法。適用於含碳粉的碳粉匣（一體式和不含感光鼓）。僅用於在普通紙上使用青色、洋紅色、黃色和黑色碳粉匣列印時的頁數產出測量。不涉及品質、可靠性等其他聲明。不適用於 A3 以上或僅限照片的印表機。針對碳粉補充系統有特定程序修改。針對辦公空間設備，不適用於生產型或大型印刷機。
