# ISO/PAS 45007:2026 — Occupational health and safety management — Risks arising from climate change and climate change action — Guidance for organizations

## L1: 基本資訊（必填）

**title**: Occupational health and safety management — Risks arising from climate change and climate change action — Guidance for organizations
**iso_refno**: ISO/PAS 45007:2026
**edition**: 1
**date**: 2026-01-14
**ics_code**: 13.100
**technical_committee**: ISO/TC 283
**source_layer**: iso_standards
**original_content**: This document gives guidance to organizations on planning for and addressing occupational health and safety (OH&S) risks arising from climate change and climate change action, including: — OH&S risks which arise as a result of climate change adaptation efforts, including changing ways of working and work processes, and infrastructure upgrades; — OH&S risks arising from climate change mitigation actions; — OH&S opportunities arising from both climate change adaptation and mitigation actions. This document is applicable to all organizations taking a systematic approach to addressing OH&S risks arising from climate change. It is applicable to organizations of all sizes including small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).

**explanation**: 此為新發布的公開可用規範（PAS），提供組織規劃和處理氣候變遷及氣候行動引起的職業健康安全（OH&S）風險的指引。涵蓋氣候適應行動（改變工作方式、流程、基礎設施升級）產生的 OH&S 風險、氣候減緩行動產生的 OH&S 風險，以及兩者帶來的機會。適用於所有規模的組織，包括中小企業。35 頁的內容反映了氣候變遷與職安交集的複雜性。

## L3: 歷史脈絡（選填）

**replaces**: N/A
**context**: 氣候變遷對工作場所安全的影響日益顯著（極端高溫、洪水、空氣品質惡化等），同時淨零轉型也帶來新的職安風險（如氫能、碳捕集技術）。本 PAS 由 ISO/TC 283（職業健康與安全管理）制定，填補了 ISO 45001 框架下氣候相關風險的指引空白。
