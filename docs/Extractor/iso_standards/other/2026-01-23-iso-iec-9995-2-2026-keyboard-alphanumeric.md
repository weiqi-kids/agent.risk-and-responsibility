# ISO/IEC 9995-2:2026 — Information technology — Keyboard layouts for text and office systems — Part 2: Alphanumeric section

## L1: 基本資訊（必填）

**title**: Information technology — Keyboard layouts for text and office systems — Part 2: Alphanumeric section
**iso_refno**: ISO/IEC 9995-2:2026
**edition**: 4
**date**: 2026-01-23
**ics_code**: 35.180
**technical_committee**: ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 35
**source_layer**: iso_standards
**original_content**: Within the general scope described in ISO/IEC 9995-1, this document specifies the alphanumeric section of a keyboard and the division of that section into zones; the arrangement, number, and location of the keys in the alphanumeric zone ZA0 of the alphanumeric section; and the layout, allocation and effect of several control functions to the keys in the function zones of the alphanumeric section.

## L2: 責任轉移判定（必填）

**shift_type**: clarified
**enforcement_signal**: recommended
**rule_type**: Published
**affected_party**: Keyboard manufacturers, layout designers, OS input system developers
**explanation**: 此為第 4 版標準，在 ISO/IEC 9995-1 框架下規範鍵盤的字母數字區段及其區域劃分。規定字母數字區 ZA0 中按鍵的排列、數量和位置，以及功能區中控制功能的配置和效果。

## L3: 歷史脈絡（選填）
