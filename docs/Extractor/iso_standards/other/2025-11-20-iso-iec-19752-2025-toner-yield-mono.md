# ISO/IEC 19752:2025 — Information technology — Office equipment — Method for the determination of toner cartridge yield for monochromatic electrophotographic printers and multi-function devices that contain printer components

## L1: 基本資訊（必填）

**title**: Information technology — Office equipment — Method for the determination of toner cartridge yield for monochromatic electrophotographic printers and multi-function devices that contain printer components
**iso_refno**: ISO/IEC 19752:2025
**edition**: 3
**date**: 2025-11-20
**ics_code**: 37.100.10
**technical_committee**: ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 28
**source_layer**: iso_standards
**original_content**: This document is limited to the evaluation of toner cartridge page yield for toner containing cartridges (i.e. all-in-one toner cartridges and toner cartridges without a photoconductor) for monochrome electrophotographic print systems. This document can also be applied to the printer component of any multifunctional device that has a digital input-printing path, including multi-function devices that contain printer components. This document is only intended for the measurement of toner cartridge yield. No other claims may be made from this testing regarding quality, reliability, etc. This document is not for use with printers whose minimum printable size is equal to or greater than A3 or for photo-only printers. Application of this document for yield measurement of toner replenishment systems implies some procedural modifications specifically noted herein. This document is intended for equipment used in the office space and does not apply to production volume or large format printing machines where the major cost of ownership is not caused by the consumable yield measured in this document.

## L2: 責任轉移判定（必填）

**shift_type**: clarified
**enforcement_signal**: recommended
**rule_type**: Published
**affected_party**: Printer manufacturers, toner cartridge vendors, procurement departments
**explanation**: 此為第 3 版標準，更新單色電子攝影列印系統的碳粉匣頁數產出測量方法。適用於含碳粉的碳粉匣（一體式碳粉匣和不含感光鼓的碳粉匣）和多功能裝置的列印元件。僅用於測量碳粉匣產出，不涉及品質、可靠性等其他聲明。不適用於 A3 以上或僅限照片的印表機。針對碳粉補充系統（內建式儲槽）有特定程序修改。針對辦公空間設備，不適用於生產型或大型印刷機。

## L3: 歷史脈絡（選填）

**replaces**: ISO/IEC 19752:2017 (standard/68555)
**context**: 碳粉匣產出是列印成本的主要組成，本標準提供了標準化測量方法，支援消費者比較和綠色採購。本次第 3 版更新反映了碳粉技術的演進（如化學碳粉、高產出碳粉匣）。
