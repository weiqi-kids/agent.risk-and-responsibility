**標準範圍**: This document specifies a subjective image quality assessment methodology that covers a range from good quality up to mathematically lossless.This document is applicable to the assessment of distortions due to image coding (i.e. lossy compression) and not necessarily other kinds of distortions (e.g. capture, sensor or rendering artefacts).
