# ISO/IEC 17629:2025 — Information technology — Office equipment — Method for measuring first print out time for digital printing devices

## L1: 基本資訊（必填）

**title**: Information technology — Office equipment — Method for measuring first print out time for digital printing devices
**iso_refno**: ISO/IEC 17629:2025
**edition**: 2
**date**: 2025-11-26
**ics_code**: 37.100.10
**technical_committee**: ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 28
**source_layer**: iso_standards
**original_content**: This document specifies a method for measuring first print out time (FPOT) of digital printing devices. The document is applicable to digital printing devices and multifunctional devices, including black and white (B&W) and colour devices of any underlying marking technology. The document includes instructions for test charts, test setup procedure, test procedure and the reporting requirements for the digital printing measurements.

## L2: 責任轉移判定（必填）

**shift_type**: clarified
**enforcement_signal**: recommended
**rule_type**: Published
**affected_party**: Printer manufacturers, testing laboratories, procurement departments
**explanation**: 此為第 2 版標準，更新了數位列印裝置首頁輸出時間（FPOT）的測量方法。適用於數位列印裝置和多功能裝置，包括任何底層標記技術的黑白和彩色裝置。包含測試圖表、測試設置程序、測試程序和數位列印測量的報告要求。

## L3: 歷史脈絡（選填）

**replaces**: ISO/IEC 17629:2015 (standard/60200)
**context**: FPOT 是評估列印裝置性能的重要指標，影響使用者體驗和能源效率。本次第 2 版更新反映了列印技術的演進（如固態墨水、LED 列印）和測量方法的精進，確保標準與現代辦公設備的一致性。
