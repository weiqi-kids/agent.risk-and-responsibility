# ISO/IEC 19583-27:2025 — Information technology — Concepts and usage of metadata — Part 27: Mapping between metamodel for computable data registration and bioinformatics analyses by high-throughput sequencing (HTS)

## L1: 基本資訊（必填）

**title**: Information technology — Concepts and usage of metadata — Part 27: Mapping between metamodel for computable data registration and bioinformatics analyses by high-throughput sequencing (HTS)
**iso_refno**: ISO/IEC 19583-27:2025
**edition**: 1
**date**: 2025-12-12
**ics_code**: 07.080, 35.240.70
**technical_committee**: ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 32
**source_layer**: iso_standards
**original_content**: This document provides a mapping between the ISO/IEC 11179-34 metamodel for computable data registration and the IEEE 2791 standard for bioinformatics analyses generated by high-throughput sequencing (HTS), to facilitate the production of IEEE 2791 objects from instances of ISO/IEC 11179-34 metamodel and the registration of IEEE 2791 objects as computable data within an MDR conforming to ISO/IEC 11179-34. This document is applicable to those who are submitting data to organizations that require metadata submissions in IEEE 2791 compliant format, as well as those aiming to register IEEE 2791 objects into an MDR that conforms to ISO/IEC 11179-34.

## L2: 責任轉移判定（必填）

**shift_type**: new

**rule_type**: Published
**affected_party**: Bioinformatics researchers, genomic data repositories, metadata registry operators
**explanation**: 此為新發布的國際標準（第 1 版），提供 ISO/IEC 11179-34（可計算資料註冊元模型）與 IEEE 2791（高通量定序生物資訊分析標準）之間的對應。促進從 ISO/IEC 11179-34 元模型實例產生 IEEE 2791 物件，以及將 IEEE 2791 物件註冊為元資料倉儲（MDR）中的可計算資料。50 頁的內容反映了兩個複雜標準之間對應的細緻性。

## L3: 歷史脈絡（選填）

**replaces**: N/A
**context**: 高通量定序（HTS）資料的元資料管理是生物醫學研究的重要挑戰。IEEE 2791（BioCompute Objects）提供了描述生物資訊分析流程的標準，ISO/IEC 11179-34 提供了元資料註冊框架。本標準建立了兩者之間的橋樑，促進基因組資料的互操作性和可重現性。
