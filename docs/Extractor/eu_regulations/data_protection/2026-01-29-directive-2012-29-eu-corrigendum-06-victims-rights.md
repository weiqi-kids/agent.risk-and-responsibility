## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Specialist professionals in criminal justice systems, Member States implementing victim protection standards, Training providers for judicial and law enforcement staff
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Corrects terminology in Directive 2012/29/EU by replacing "public officials" with "specialist professionals" in training requirements for staff handling crime victims, clarifying scope of professionals subject to training obligations.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Data Protection (victim information handling)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Specialist professionals working with crime victims must receive appropriate training
  - Training must cover identification of victims' needs
  - Training must ensure respectful, sensitive, professional and non-discriminatory treatment
  - Member States must ensure training provisions are implemented for specialist staff
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Training program documentation for specialist professionals
  - Training completion records
  - Competency assessment records for staff handling victims
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
