## Notes

This corrigendum addresses five technical corrections to Regulation (EU) 2024/1347, which establishes uniform standards for recognition of international protection beneficiaries across the EU. The original regulation replaces Directive 2011/95/EU.

**Most significant correction**: The change from "same conditions" to "equivalent conditions" in Article 34(1) provides Member States with necessary flexibility in accommodation provision while maintaining protection standards.

**Categorization rationale**: Classified under data_protection as it concerns protection of vulnerable persons' rights and processing of personal data in international protection procedures, though it also intersects with critical_infrastructure (public services) and ai_governance (automated decision systems in migration procedures).

**Original regulation context**: Regulation (EU) 2024/1347 was adopted on 14 May 2024 and published on 22 May 2024 in OJ L 2024/1347.
