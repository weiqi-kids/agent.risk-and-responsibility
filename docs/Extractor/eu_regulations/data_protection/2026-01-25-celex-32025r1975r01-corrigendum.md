## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**: Corrigendum corrects Bulgarian language version of Article 14a(2)(b) from "residential heating or capacity" to "residential heating or electricity". All parties applying exemptions under Article 14a(2)(b) for natural gas purchases in Iran must use the corrected text. The corrigendum has retroactive effect from the original regulation's entry into force (2025-09-29). Member State citizens, diplomatic missions, and authorities must confirm the exemption scope applies to residential heating or electricity purposes. Compliance records must follow original Article 14a requirements. Entities should review existing energy procurement compliance procedures to ensure correct text application.
- **evidence_requirements**: Exemption documentation as required by original Article 14a provisions (corrigendum does not change documentation standards, only corrects text). Member State competent authority approval documents where applicable. Records of transactions applying the exemption must reflect corrected text scope (residential heating or electricity).
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
