## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: General Secretariat of the Council (data controller), Data Protection Officer, data subjects under restrictive measures, EU institutions, Member States
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Establishes implementing rules for restricting GDPR-equivalent data subject rights when processing personal data for EU restrictive measures (sanctions)

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Data protection compliance
- Sanctions enforcement
- Transparency vs. security balance
- Individual rights limitations

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - GSC must conduct necessity and proportionality testing before restricting data subject rights
  - Maintain records of all restrictions applied
  - Review restrictions annually
  - Inform Data Protection Officer of all restrictions
  - Provide data subjects with general privacy information about potential restrictions
  - Provide principal reasons when restrictions are applied (unless disclosure would undermine effectiveness)
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Documented necessity/proportionality assessments
  - Records of restrictions maintained by the controller
  - Annual review documentation
  - Privacy notices to data subjects
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This decision implements Article 25(9) of Regulation (EU) 2018/1725, establishing procedural safeguards when the Council restricts data subject rights (e.g., access, rectification, erasure) during preparation and implementation of EU sanctions regimes.
