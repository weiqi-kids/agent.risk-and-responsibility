## Notes
This is the 5th corrigendum to Regulation (EU) 2024/1183, which amends the foundational eIDAS Regulation (EU) No 910/2014. The correction does not concern the English version. The corrigendum specifically addresses Article 1, amendment point (43)(c), correcting only the grammatical form of "registered" in the Maltese translation while maintaining technical substance. This regulation establishes the European Digital Identity Framework and interoperability requirements for qualified trust services.
