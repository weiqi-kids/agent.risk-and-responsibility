## Notes
This is a technical corrigendum (Berichtigung) correcting 15 minor errors in the original Directive 2024/1500 published on 29 May 2024. Corrections primarily address German-language terminology consistency (e.g., "Funktionsweise" to "Arbeitsweise", "Gleichstellung der Geschlechter" to "Gleichstellungsaspekt") and grammatical improvements across recitals and articles. The corrigendum ensures uniform interpretation of equality body standards across all language versions. Original directive concerns standards for equality bodies handling gender equality and non-discrimination complaints in employment contexts.
