## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Complete CCT duty suspensions for products with inadequate EU production
  - Partial suspensions for battery-related products with mandatory review by 31 December 2026
  - Updated technical descriptions, classifications, and end-use requirements for existing entries
  - New review dates for strategically important products
  - Removal of products no longer benefiting the Union effective 1 January 2026
  - Application of amended Annex effective 1 January 2026
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - CN codes and TARIC classifications documentation
  - Justification for inadequate EU production claims
  - Battery production support compliance verification
  - Strategic importance assessment for review date establishment
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This regulation modifies Regulation (EU) 2021/2278, which suspends Common Customs Tariff duties under Article 56(2)(c) of Regulation (EU) No 952/2013. The amended Annex contains extensive product listings with CN codes, TARIC classifications, duty rates (predominantly 0% or partial percentages), and specific mandatory review dates extending to 31 December 2030. Application date is 1 January 2026.
