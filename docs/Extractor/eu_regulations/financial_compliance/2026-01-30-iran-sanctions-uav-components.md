## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU exporters, customs authorities, dual-use technology suppliers, export control officers
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Extends export prohibitions to Iran by adding components used in developing UAVs and missiles across 10 categories including energetic materials, electronics, machine tools, sensors, navigation systems, and aerospace components

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Financial compliance
- Export controls
- Sanctions enforcement
- Supply chain security

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Prohibit export to Iran of newly listed dual-use technology and military components
  - Screen transactions for controlled items across 10 material categories
  - Implement export control procedures for energetic substances, integrated circuits, flight control systems
  - Ensure compliance with updated Annex II controlled items list
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Export authorization records
  - Item classification documentation
  - Customs declaration records
  - Compliance verification documentation
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Amends Regulation (EU) 2023/1529 beyond restrictions established in July 2023 and May 2024. Covers materials ranging from raw energetic substances to sophisticated integrated circuits and flight control systems.
