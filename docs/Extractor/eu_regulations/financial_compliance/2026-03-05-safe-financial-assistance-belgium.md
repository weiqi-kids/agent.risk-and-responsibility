- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Makes available to Belgium a loan of up to EUR 8,340,027,698 under the SAFE instrument for defence industry investments, with EUR 1,251,004,154.70 as initial pre-financing.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Belgium must ensure defence investments comply with Article 16 of Regulation (EU) 2025/1106 (SAFE)
  - Activities must involve common or single procurements improving defence product availability or ensuring interoperability across the Union
  - Belgium must protect EU financial interests and comply with Union procurement rules
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Commission assessment confirming Belgium's request fulfils all applicable conditions
  - Documentation of procurement compliance and interoperability measures
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
