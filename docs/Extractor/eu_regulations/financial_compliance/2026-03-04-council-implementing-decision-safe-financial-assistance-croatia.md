## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Republic of Croatia, European Commission, Croatian defence procurement entities, EU financial institutions
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: EU financial assistance under the SAFE (Security Action for Europe) instrument is made available to Croatia with a maximum loan of EUR 1.7 billion and EUR 255 million in pre-financing for defence industry investment.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Defence industry financing and procurement compliance
- EU financial instrument governance (Regulation (EU) 2025/1106)
- National defence investment and EU financial safeguards

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Croatia must invest SAFE funds in defence products and related expenditures via common or single procurements
  - Activities must address structural industry changes, improve defence product availability, or ensure EU-wide interoperability
  - Croatia must comply with procurement rules under Regulation (EU) 2025/1106
  - Loan agreement must include financial safeguards protecting EU interests
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Croatia's request and investment plan must meet regulatory requirements
  - Compliance documentation for procurement rules
  - Financial safeguard provisions in the loan agreement
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
