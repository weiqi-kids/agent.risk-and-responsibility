- **shift_summary**: Makes available to Poland a loan of up to EUR 43,734,100,805 under the SAFE instrument for defence industry investments, with EUR 6,560,115,120.75 as initial pre-financing.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Poland must ensure defence investments comply with Article 16 of Regulation (EU) 2025/1106 (SAFE)
  - Activities must involve common or single procurements aimed at speeding up defence industry structural adjustment
  - Poland must protect EU financial interests and comply with EU budget regulations
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Commission confirmation that Poland's request fulfils all applicable conditions
  - Documentation of interoperability objectives and procurement compliance
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
