## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Republic of Poland (recipient), European Commission (implementing authority), EU defence industry entities
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Unlocks up to EUR 43.7 billion in concessional SAFE instrument loans for Poland to fund European defence industry investment, subject to compliance with procurement and financial protection conditions under Regulation (EU) 2025/1106.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Defence industry financing and procurement
- EU financial assistance conditionality
- Supply chain resilience for defence products

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Poland must comply with Regulation (EU) 2025/1106, particularly regarding defence product procurement and industry adjustment measures
  - Activities must involve common or single procurements addressing defence industry structural changes or product interoperability
  - Compliance with Article 16 standards and applicable procurement rules required
  - Loan agreement must include appropriate measures for protection of EU financial interests
  - Pre-financing of EUR 6,560,115,120.75 available
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Poland must submit a European Defence Industry Investment Plan
  - Commission assessment confirms fulfilment of conditions applying principles of equal treatment, solidarity, proportionality, and transparency
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
