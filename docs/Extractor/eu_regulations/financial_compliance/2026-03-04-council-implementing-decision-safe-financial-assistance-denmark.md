## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Kingdom of Denmark, European Commission, Danish defence procurement entities, EU financial institutions
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: EU financial assistance under the SAFE (Security Action for Europe) instrument is made available to Denmark with a maximum loan of EUR 46,796,822, of which EUR 7,019,523.30 is provided as pre-financing for defence procurement.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Defence industry financing and procurement compliance
- EU financial instrument governance (Regulation (EU) 2025/1106)
- National defence investment and EU financial safeguards

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Denmark must invest SAFE funds in defence products or other products for defence purposes via common or single procurements
  - Denmark must comply with procurement rules set out in Regulation (EU) 2025/1106
  - Loan agreement must include measures protecting EU financial interests
  - Pre-financing of EUR 7,019,523.30 must be applied according to approved investment plan
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Denmark's investment plan must demonstrate compliance with regulatory requirements for defence product procurement
  - Documentation of common or single procurement procedures
  - Financial safeguards protecting EU interests in the loan agreement
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
