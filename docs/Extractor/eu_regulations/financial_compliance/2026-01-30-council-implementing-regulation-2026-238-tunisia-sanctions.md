## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU Member States, Financial institutions, Designated individuals (27 persons), National competent authorities
- **shift_type**: restricted
- **shift_summary**: Removes three individuals from Tunisia sanctions list and updates procedural documentation for 24 persons, reducing scope of restrictive measures under Regulation (EU) No 101/2011.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Financial Compliance (sanctions enforcement)
- Data Protection (personal information of designated persons)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Member States must remove sanctions on three delisted individuals
  - Member States must update records for 24 persons with rights of defence information
  - Financial institutions must unfreeze assets of delisted individuals
  - National authorities must ensure compliance with updated sanctions list
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated sanctions list implementation records
  - Asset unfreezing documentation for three delisted persons
  - Procedural documentation updates for 24 persons under Tunisian law
