- **shift_summary**: Makes available to Slovakia a loan of up to EUR 2,316,674,361 under the SAFE (Security Action for Europe) instrument for defence industry investments, with EUR 347,501,154.15 as initial pre-financing.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Slovakia must ensure defence investments comply with Article 16 of Regulation (EU) 2025/1106 (SAFE)
  - Activities must involve common or single procurements meeting EU interoperability standards
  - Slovakia must protect EU financial interests and comply with Union procurement rules
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Commission assessment confirming Slovakia's request fulfils all applicable conditions
  - Compliance documentation for procurement and interoperability requirements
