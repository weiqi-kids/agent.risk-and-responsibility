## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU guarantee ceiling increased to EUR 29.05 billion (up from previous levels set in earlier amendments)
  - Implementing partners must obtain Investment Committee approval for all financing operations
  - Annual reporting required on operations supported under the InvestEU Programme
  - Member States may establish compartment arrangements with contribution agreements
  - New InvestEU financial instrument available under Member State compartment
  - Simplified SME definition to reduce administrative complexity
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Annual reports on financing operations (simplified for operations under EUR 300,000)
  - Key performance indicators (KPIs) tracking (exemptions for small operations)
  - Investment Committee approval documentation
  - Member State contribution agreements (where applicable)
  - Compliance with strategic European investment priorities (green, digital, housing, defense, transport, R&D)
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
此法規修正 InvestEU 方案架構（Regulation (EU) 2021/523 及相關法規），目的為回應地緣政治變化（烏克蘭戰爭、中東局勢）與歐盟 2024-2029 政治議程，增加公共投資撬動民間資金能力。主要變更包括：(1) 擔保上限提高至 290.5 億歐元；(2) 新增成員國隔間機制以擴大財務工具；(3) 簡化小型營運報告要求（30 萬歐元以下免除部分 KPI）。合約簽署期限延至 2028 年 12 月 31 日。投資優先領域包括綠色轉型、數位化、住房、國防科技與工業基礎（DTIB）、運輸、研發創新。

**注意**：原檔案誤將此法規標記為「ENISA 管理式資安服務法規」，實際上此法規與網路安全無關，為金融投資工具法規。此次重新萃取已更正分類至 financial_compliance。
