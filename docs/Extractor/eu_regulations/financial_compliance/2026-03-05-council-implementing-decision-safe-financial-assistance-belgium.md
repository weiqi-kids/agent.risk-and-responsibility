## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Kingdom of Belgium (recipient), European Commission (implementing authority), EU defence industry entities
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Unlocks up to EUR 8.34 billion in concessional SAFE instrument loans for Belgium to support European defence industry investment, conditional on compliance with Regulation (EU) 2025/1106 procurement and financial protection requirements.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Defence industry financing and procurement
- EU financial assistance conditionality
- Supply chain resilience for defence products

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Belgium must use the loan for defence products and related purposes through common or single procurements
  - Compliance with Article 16 of Regulation (EU) 2025/1106 and applicable procurement rules required
  - Activities must support industry structural adjustment, timely defence product availability, or EU-wide interoperability
  - Loan agreement must include measures for protection of EU financial interests
  - Pre-financing of EUR 1,251,004,154.70 available
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Belgium submitted a European Defence Industry Investment Plan on 28 November 2025
  - Commission assessment confirms fulfilment of conditions under Regulation (EU) 2025/1106
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
