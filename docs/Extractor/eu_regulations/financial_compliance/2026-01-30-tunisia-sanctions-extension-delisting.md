## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Continue implementing restrictive measures against listed persons and entities until 31 January 2027
  - Remove three delisted individuals from sanctions enforcement
  - Update internal records with revised legal protection information for 24 persons
  - Ensure fair trial procedures and judicial protections are respected per amended Annex
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated sanctions lists reflecting delistings
  - Asset freeze implementation records
  - Documentation of rights of defence and judicial protection provisions
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This decision amends Decision 2011/72/CFSP concerning restrictive measures in view of the situation in Tunisia. The decision extends the existing sanctions framework by one year and makes administrative updates to reflect current legal proceedings and protections. The three delistings and updated information for 24 persons reflect evolving judicial proceedings in Tunisia.
