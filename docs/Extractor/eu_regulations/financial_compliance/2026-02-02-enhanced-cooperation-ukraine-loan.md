## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: 24 participating Member States (Belgium, Bulgaria, Denmark, Germany, Estonia, Ireland, Greece, Spain, France, Croatia, Italy, Cyprus, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Austria, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovenia, Finland, Sweden), 3 non-participating Member States (Czech Republic, Hungary, Slovakia), Ukraine (loan recipient), European Commission
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Establishes enhanced cooperation mechanism among 24 Member States to provide €90 billion loan to Ukraine for 2026-2027, with financial obligations excluding non-participating states

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Participating Member States bear financial expenditures for the loan facility
  - Participating Member States provide guarantee coverage for contingent liabilities
  - Non-participating Member States receive budget contribution adjustments (exempted from financing)
  - Loan funded through EU capital market borrowing backed by budget headroom
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Financial contribution records from participating states
  - Budget adjustment documentation for non-participating states
  - Guarantee coverage documentation
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This enhanced cooperation decision follows Article 20 TEU and Articles 326-334 TFEU, allowing a subset of Member States to proceed with closer integration when unanimity cannot be achieved. The €90 billion loan spans 2026-2027. Czech Republic, Hungary, and Slovakia do not participate and are exempt from financing obligations.
