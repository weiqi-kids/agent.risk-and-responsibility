## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Slovak Republic (recipient), European Commission (implementing authority), EU defence industry entities
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Unlocks up to EUR 2.32 billion in concessional SAFE instrument loans for Slovakia to support European defence industry investment, conditional on compliance with Regulation (EU) 2025/1106 procurement and financial protection requirements.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Defence industry financing and procurement
- EU financial assistance conditionality
- Supply chain resilience for defence products

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Slovakia must comply with Articles 4, 7(2), and 16 of Regulation (EU) 2025/1106
  - Loan proceeds must be used for defence products or defence-related products via common or single procurements
  - Loan agreement must include measures for protection of EU financial interests
  - Pre-financing of EUR 347,501,154.15 available upon compliance verification
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Slovak Republic must submit a European Defence Industry Investment Plan
  - Commission assessment of compliance with regulatory conditions required prior to disbursement
  - Compliance with applicable procurement rules mandatory
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
