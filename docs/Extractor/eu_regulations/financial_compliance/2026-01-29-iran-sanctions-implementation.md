## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **shift_summary**: Adds four persons and six entities to sanctions list for involvement in Iran's ballistic missile and UAV supply to Russia and support for military equipment transfers

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Apply travel bans and asset freezes to newly designated persons and entities
  - Update sanctions screening systems with Annex III amendments
  - Prohibit transactions with designated entities involved in Iran's military cooperation with Russia
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Sanctions list compliance records
  - Asset freeze documentation
  - Transaction screening records
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Implements Regulation (EU) 2023/1529. Targets Iran's ballistic missile development, UAV manufacturing and transfer, and supply chain support for military equipment.
