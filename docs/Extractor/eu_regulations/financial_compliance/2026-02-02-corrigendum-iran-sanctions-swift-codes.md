## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Financial institutions, sanctions compliance officers, customs authorities, Member State competent authorities
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Corrects SWIFT code formatting errors in Iran sanctions list to ensure accurate identification of designated entities

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Financial sanctions compliance
- Banking identification accuracy
- Cross-border payment monitoring

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Use corrected SWIFT codes for sanctions screening (Entry 156: "SCERIRTHKSH" not "SCERIRTH KSH"; Entry 173: "REFAIRTH" not "REF AIRTH")
  - Update internal sanctions lists with corrected identifiers
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated sanctions screening databases
  - Transaction monitoring records using corrected codes
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Technical correction to Council Implementing Regulation (EU) 2025/1980 implementing EU sanctions against Iran. Removes spaces from two SWIFT codes to ensure proper formatting. This corrigendum inherits the binding force of the original regulation (directly applicable).
