## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU Member States, financial institutions, entities involved in Tunisia-related transactions, sanctions compliance officers
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Technical correction to Annex I heading reference (Article 2 instead of Article 1) in Tunisia sanctions list, clarifying the legal basis for designated persons and entities

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Financial compliance (restrictive measures / sanctions)
- Cross-border transaction monitoring

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Apply corrected reference to Annex I when identifying designated persons and entities under Tunisia sanctions regime
  - Update internal compliance documentation to reflect correct article reference
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Maintain accurate records of sanctions screening against corrected Annex I designation list
  - Document compliance procedures referencing correct legal provisions
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This is a technical corrigendum correcting a numbering error in the annex heading. The substantive sanctions obligations remain unchanged. The English version was not affected by this corrigendum.
