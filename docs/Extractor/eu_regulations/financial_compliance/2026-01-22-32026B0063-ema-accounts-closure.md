## L1 — Rule Signal

- **document_type**: Decision
- **binding_force**: directly_applicable

**法規全名**：Decision (EU) 2026/63 of the European Parliament of 22 October 2025 on the closure of the accounts of the European Union Agency for Asylum for the financial year 2023

**文件類型**：Decision（歐洲議會決議）

**生效日期**：2026-01-13（官方公報發布日期）

**CELEX 編號**：32026B0063

**執行層級**：EU-wide（歐盟層級）

**管轄對象**：European Union Agency for Asylum 及歐盟預算監督機構

**主要義務**：批准 EU Agency for Asylum 2023 財務年度帳目結算，並通知相關機構

**頒布機構**：European Parliament

## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **affected_roles**: European Parliament (discharge authority), Executive Director of EU Agency for Asylum, Court of Auditors, European Commission, Council of the European Union
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: This decision formally closes the 2023 financial year accounts of the EU Agency for Asylum, completing the discharge procedure after the Parliament's initial postponement decision on 2025-05-07, thereby clarifying the final accountability status for that financial period.

## L3 — Risk Domains

**財務透明度風險**
- 本決議結束帳目結算程序，顯示先前存在延期審查情況（Council 於 2025-02-17 提出建議，議會於 2025-05-07 延期），暗示該機構財務審計可能曾存在爭議點
- 風險層級：低（帳目最終獲批准，未見罰則或財務不當紀錄）

**機構問責風險**
- 歐盟庇護署作為專責機構，其財務管理透明度直接影響公眾信任
- 風險層級：低（決議未提及任何違規或缺失）

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence

- **new_obligations**: Parliament President must forward this decision to the EU Agency for Asylum, Council, Commission, and Court of Auditors; decision must be published in the Official Journal of the European Union
- **evidence_requirements**: Official Journal publication record (L series, 2026-01-13), reception records from notified institutions demonstrating procedural completion
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes

**文件性質**：此為歐盟常規預算監督程序的一部分，屬於「discharge procedure」（解除責任程序）。雖然文件顯示帳目結算曾延期審查，但最終獲批准表明無重大財務違規。

**系統意義**：EU Agency for Asylum 負責協調成員國庇護申請程序，其財務透明度對維持歐盟庇護體系公信力至關重要。本決議確認該機構 2023 年度財務管理符合規範。

**與其他監管的關聯**：此類帳目結算決議為歐盟所有機構的年度常規程序，Court of Auditors 的審計報告通常為議會決議的參考依據。
