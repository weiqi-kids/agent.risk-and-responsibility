- **binding_force**: directly_applicable

## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: 23 participating Member States (Belgium, Bulgaria, Denmark, Germany, Estonia, Ireland, Greece, Spain, France, Croatia, Italy, Cyprus, Latvia, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Netherlands, Austria, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovenia, Finland, Sweden), EU financial institutions, Ukraine as beneficiary
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: 23 Member States undertake new financial obligations to guarantee a €90 billion loan to Ukraine for 2026-2027

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Financial compliance (loan guarantee obligations)
- Supply chain (support for critical infrastructure in conflict zone)
- Critical infrastructure (reconstruction financing)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Participating Member States must contribute financially to enhanced cooperation expenditures
  - Participating Member States must guarantee contingent liabilities linked to the €90 billion loan
  - Non-participating Member States (Czech Republic, Hungary, Slovakia) receive budget adjustments and are excluded from financing obligations
  - Other Member States may join the cooperation under specified terms at any time
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Financial contribution records
  - Guarantee documentation
  - Budget adjustment accounting for non-participating states
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
此決定基於《歐盟條約》第 20 條和《歐盟運作條約》第 212 條，由 2025 年 12 月 18 日歐洲理事會結論授權。非參與國（捷克、匈牙利、斯洛伐克）明確排除在融資義務之外。
