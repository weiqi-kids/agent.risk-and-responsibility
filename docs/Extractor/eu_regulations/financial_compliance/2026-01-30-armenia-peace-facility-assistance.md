## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Republic of Armenia Armed Forces, EU CFSP implementing bodies, asset monitoring authorities
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Provides EUR 20 million over 35 months for non-lethal logistics and medical equipment to scale Armenia's battalion-size tent camp and medical facility to brigade level, subject to compliance, security, and reporting obligations

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Financial compliance
- International humanitarian law
- Asset management

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Ensure Armed Forces compliance with international human rights law and international humanitarian law
  - Use assets properly and efficiently for intended purposes with adequate maintenance
  - Prevent asset loss or unauthorized transfer
  - Submit annual inventory reports on designated items
  - Grant access for on-site checks and audits
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Annual inventory documentation
  - Asset use and maintenance records
  - Audit access records
  - Compliance verification documentation
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Duration: 35 months from adoption. Scope limited to non-lethal logistics and medical equipment, supplies, services, and technical training.
