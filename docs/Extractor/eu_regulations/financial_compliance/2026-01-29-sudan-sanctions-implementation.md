## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **shift_summary**: Adds seven individuals to sanctions list for serious human rights violations, international humanitarian law breaches, and undermining Sudan's stability and political transition

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Apply travel bans and asset freezes to seven newly designated individuals
  - Update sanctions screening systems with Annex I amendments
  - Enforce restrictions against RSF commanders, SAF-affiliated militia leaders, and facilitators of targeting campaigns
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Sanctions list compliance records
  - Travel ban enforcement documentation
  - Asset freeze records
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Implements Regulation (EU) 2023/2147. Targets individuals involved in El Fasher atrocities (October 2025) and systematic violence in Gezirah and Sennar States.
