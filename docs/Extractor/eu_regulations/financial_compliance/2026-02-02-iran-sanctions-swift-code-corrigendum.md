## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU Member States, financial institutions, sanctions compliance officers, banking systems, payment processors
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Technical correction of SWIFT/BIC codes in Iran sanctions designation list (entries 156 and 173), ensuring accurate identification of designated entities in financial systems

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Update sanctions screening systems with corrected SWIFT codes (entry 156: SCERIRTHKSH; entry 173: REFAIRTH)
  - Re-screen existing transactions and counterparties against corrected identifiers
  - Maintain audit trail of system updates
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Documentation of sanctions list updates in compliance systems
  - Records of corrected bank identification codes implementation
  - Transaction screening logs using accurate SWIFT codes
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This corrigendum corrects technical formatting errors in bank identification codes (SWIFT/BIC codes) within the Iran sanctions annexes. Entry 156 corrected from "SCERIRTH KSH" to "SCERIRTHKSH" and entry 173 from "REF AIRTH" to "REFAIRTH". These corrections are critical for accurate automated sanctions screening in financial systems.
