## L2: 權責轉移分析

**affected_roles**:
- EFTA States (Iceland, Liechtenstein, Norway)
- Central counterparties (CCPs) in EU and EFTA States
- Competent authorities (financial supervisors)
- Resolution authorities
- EFTA Surveillance Authority

**shift_type**: Authority → Supervision

**shift_summary**: Decision establishes EU position for EEA Joint Committee to integrate central counterparty (CCP) recovery and resolution framework into EEA Agreement by amending Annexes IX (Financial services), XII (Free movement of capital), and XXII (Company law). This extends EU CCP regulatory framework to EFTA States, requiring CCPs to implement recovery plans subject to supervisory assessment and granting authorities suspension powers over OTC derivatives clearing obligations.

## L4: 操作衝擊

### new_obligations

**For Central Counterparties (CCPs)**:
- Develop and maintain comprehensive recovery plans
- Submit recovery plans to competent authorities for assessment
- Implement recovery measures as specified in approved plans
- Comply with factors specified in Commission Delegated Regulation 2023/451
- Maintain operational resilience during stress scenarios

**For Competent Authorities**:
- Assess CCP recovery plans for adequacy and credibility
- Supervise CCP implementation of recovery measures
- Exercise suspension powers over clearing obligations for OTC derivatives under specified conditions
- Coordinate with resolution authorities and EFTA Surveillance Authority

**For Resolution Authorities**:
- Prepare resolution plans for CCPs
- Exercise resolution powers when recovery measures insufficient
- Coordinate cross-border resolution actions

**For EFTA Surveillance Authority**:
- Gain decision-making authority paralleling EU Commission powers
- Monitor compliance of EFTA State CCPs with framework

### evidence_requirements

**Recovery Plan Documentation**:
- Detailed recovery scenarios and stress testing results
- Recovery options with quantified impact assessments
- Governance arrangements for recovery implementation
- Communication plans for stakeholders
- Factors specified in Commission Delegated Regulation 2023/451

**Supervisory Assessment Records**:
- Competent authority review documentation
- Approval/rejection decisions with reasoning
- Ongoing monitoring reports

### enforcement_signal

**Enforcement Mechanism**: EEA Joint Committee coordination; EFTA Surveillance Authority gains decision-making authority paralleling EU Commission powers.

**Enforcement Authority**:
- Competent authorities (financial supervisors in each jurisdiction)
- Resolution authorities
- EFTA Surveillance Authority

**Entry Condition**: "Provided that all notifications under Article 103(1) of the EEA Agreement have been made"

**Compliance Timeline**: Upon entry into force of EEA Joint Committee decision (pending notifications)

**Risk Level**: High - systemic importance of CCPs requires stringent compliance; failure to maintain adequate recovery plans may result in supervisory intervention or resolution actions
