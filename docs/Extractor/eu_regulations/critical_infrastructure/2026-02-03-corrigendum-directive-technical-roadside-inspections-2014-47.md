## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: roadside inspection authorities, commercial vehicle operators, member state enforcement bodies, technical inspection centers
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Standardizes terminology across Directive 2014/47/EU by replacing "stanica technickej kontroly" with "kontrolné centrum" (inspection center), "zabezpečenie nákladu" with "upevnenie nákladu" (cargo fastening), and updating technical inspection certificate terminology across all annexes

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Commercial vehicle safety
- Roadside inspection procedures
- Transportation infrastructure security
- Technical compliance enforcement

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Apply updated terminology for inspection centers (kontrolné centrum)
  - Use standardized cargo fastening terminology (upevnenie nákladu)
  - Issue inspection certificates using corrected nomenclature (osvedčenie o kontrole technického stavu)
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Technical inspection certificates with corrected terminology
  - Updated Annex II specifications for braking equipment and axle pins
  - Revised Annexes III, IV, and V reflecting cargo fastening procedures
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This is the 8th corrigendum to Directive 2014/47/EU. The correction does not concern the English version. The corrigendum implements systematic terminology changes across ten sections to ensure consistency in technical language for vehicle inspection procedures, particularly affecting Slovak language versions but maintaining technical standards across all EU member states.
