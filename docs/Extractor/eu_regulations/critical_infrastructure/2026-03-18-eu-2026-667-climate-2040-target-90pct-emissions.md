## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: All EU Member States, European industries (particularly energy-intensive sectors and SMEs), European Commission, citizens throughout the Union
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Amends the European Climate Law (Regulation (EU) 2021/1119) to establish a binding EU intermediate climate target of 90% net greenhouse gas emission reduction compared to 1990 levels by 2040, as a milestone toward 2050 climate neutrality.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Climate and environmental regulation for energy-intensive industries
- Critical infrastructure transition (energy systems, transport)
- Emissions trading and carbon market policy

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Member States must align policies to achieve 90% emission reduction compared to 1990 by 2040
  - Commission must review relevant legislation and propose amendments to enable target achievement
  - Operation of emissions trading for buildings and road transport postponed until 2028
  - Commission must assess implementation every two years starting 6 March 2027
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Biennial Commission assessment reports required
  - Potential legislative adjustments based on monitoring results
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
