## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU financial institutions, compliance officers, supervisory authorities implementing Iran sanctions
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Technical correction of SWIFT codes in Iran sanctions list (Entry 156 and 173) to ensure accurate entity identification

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Critical infrastructure (financial system integrity)
- Financial compliance (sanctions enforcement)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Update internal sanctions databases with corrected SWIFT codes
  - Entry 156: Use "SCERIRTHKSH" instead of "SCERIRTH KSH"
  - Entry 173: Use "REFAIRTH" instead of "REF AIRTH"
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated sanctions screening systems
  - Transaction monitoring records reflecting corrected identifiers
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
此為技術性更正文件，僅修正原決定中的 SWIFT 代碼排版錯誤，不改變實質制裁內容。所有受制裁實體清單的系統必須更新以確保準確識別。
