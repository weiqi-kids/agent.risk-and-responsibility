  - Financial discharge of Secretariat Director

  - Secretariat organizational structure updates
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Documentation of treaty breaches by member countries
  - Budget compliance verification
  - Organizational structure justification
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This decision authorizes the EU position for the Energy Community's 23rd Ministerial Council meeting on 18 December 2025. Minor modifications may be incorporated based on feedback without requiring additional Council approval. Subsequent adoption by correspondence is permitted if the Council cannot finalize acts during the scheduled meeting.
