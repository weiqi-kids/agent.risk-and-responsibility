## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: European Commission (negotiating mandate), Ukraine (as negotiating party), EU Space Programme participants, GOVSATCOM service providers, Union Secure Connectivity Programme operators
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: The EU authorises the Commission to negotiate with Ukraine regarding Ukraine's participation in GOVSATCOM and the Union Secure Connectivity Programme, establishing the basis for Ukraine's access to EU governmental satellite communication services.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Satellite communications infrastructure, space programme security, governmental connectivity resilience

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - European Commission must negotiate an agreement covering Ukraine's participation in GOVSATCOM component of the Union Space Programme
  - Negotiation must address financial contribution calculations, participation rules (without decision-making authority for Ukraine), security protections for classified information, and EU ownership of secure connectivity assets
  - Ukraine's access to GOVSATCOM services and governmental services of the Union Secure Connectivity Programme to be defined
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Negotiating mandate documentation
  - Progress reports on negotiations
  - Security assessment documentation for classified information handling
  - Financial contribution modelling
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
