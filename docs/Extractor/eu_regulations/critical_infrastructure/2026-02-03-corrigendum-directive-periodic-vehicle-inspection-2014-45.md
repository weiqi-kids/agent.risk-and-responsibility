## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: periodic inspection centers, motor vehicle owners, member state vehicle safety authorities, testing facilities, brake system inspectors
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Standardizes terminology throughout Directive 2014/45/EU by replacing "testing station" with "inspection center," "inspection report" with "inspection certificate," and updating odometer terminology, plus technical corrections in brake system inspection procedures

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Motor vehicle safety
- Periodic inspection procedures
- Brake system compliance
- Technical testing standards

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Apply updated terminology for inspection centers (replacing "testing station")
  - Issue inspection certificates (replacing "inspection report")
  - Use standardized odometer terminology
  - Follow corrected technical language in Annex I testing procedures
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Inspection certificates with corrected nomenclature
  - Annex I compliance for brake system testing procedures
  - Annex II certificate requirements with updated terminology
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This is the 8th corrigendum to Directive 2014/45/EU. The correction does not concern the English version. The corrigendum primarily addresses linguistic and nomenclatural amendments to ensure consistent terminology across all language versions, with specific technical corrections in annexes related to brake system inspections and axle pin terminology.
