## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Italian Republic, European Commission, defence industry entities in Italy
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Italy is granted access to up to EUR 14,900,000,000 in SAFE loan assistance for defence industry investment, subject to compliance with Regulation (EU) 2025/1106 procurement and interoperability conditions.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Critical infrastructure (defence industry investment and procurement)
- Financial compliance (loan conditions, EU financial interest protections)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Italy must implement a European defence industry investment plan focused on common or single procurement frameworks
  - Activities must target structural adjustment of the defence industry, improve defence industry competitiveness, or ensure EU-wide interoperability
  - Compliance with procurement rules and financial protection measures under Regulation (EU) 2025/1106
  - Italy's request was submitted 30 November 2025 and assessed against SAFE conditions
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Investment plan demonstrating alignment with SAFE instrument objectives
  - Compliance documentation per Regulation (EU) 2025/1106
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
