## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Republic of Armenia (Armed Forces), EU High Representative, Political and Security Committee, Facility Committee, EU Member States
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Establishes financial assistance obligation for EU to provide €20 million for Armenia's military logistics and medical capabilities, with corresponding compliance, monitoring, and audit obligations for Armenia.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Critical Infrastructure (military logistics and medical facilities)
- Supply Chain Responsibility (equipment procurement and transfer controls)
- Financial Compliance (audit and monitoring requirements)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU to provide €20 million financial support for logistics and medical equipment
  - Armenia must ensure armed forces comply with international humanitarian law
  - Armenia must maintain provided assets properly
  - Armenia must prevent unauthorized transfers of equipment
  - Armenia must grant access for monitoring and audits
  - Implementation duration: 35 months from adoption
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Documentation of armed forces compliance with international humanitarian law
  - Asset maintenance records
  - Transfer control documentation
  - Audit access and cooperation records
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
