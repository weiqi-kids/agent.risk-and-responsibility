## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU member states, six Russian public figures (media presenters, journalists, actors, dancers), EU financial institutions, sanctions compliance officers, border control authorities
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Adds six Russian media and cultural figures to sanctions list for spreading propaganda, disinformation about Ukraine, and supporting Russian government policies undermining democratic values

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:

  - Financial institutions must identify and block transactions involving listed persons
  - Travel ban enforcement by border control authorities
  - Compliance reporting requirements
- **evidence_requirements**:
