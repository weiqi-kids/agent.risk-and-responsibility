## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Kingdom of Norway, European Union, national authorities of EU Member States, GOVSATCOM programme participants, Union Space Programme governance bodies
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: The EU is authorised to sign an agreement enabling Norway to participate in the GOVSATCOM component of the Union Space Programme and the Union Secure Connectivity Programme, and to access associated governmental satellite communication services.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU to sign international agreement with Norway on GOVSATCOM and Secure Connectivity Programme participation
  - Norway to comply with rules of participation in GOVSATCOM and Union Space Programme components
  - Norway to gain access to GOVSATCOM services and governmental services of the Union Secure Connectivity Programme
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Signed agreement between EU and Kingdom of Norway as legal basis for participation
  - Compliance with GOVSATCOM access and usage rules
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
WebFetch successful via /EN/TXT/ format. This decision authorises signature of an international agreement (not the agreement itself entering into force). GOVSATCOM provides secure satellite communications for governmental users; the Union Secure Connectivity Programme (IRIS²) is the EU's strategic satellite constellation for governmental and commercial secure connectivity. Norway's participation strengthens European strategic space infrastructure beyond EU borders, relevant to critical infrastructure resilience.
