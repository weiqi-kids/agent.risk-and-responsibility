## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU financial institutions, asset management entities, supervisory authorities, delisted individuals (Lilia Bent Noureddine Ben Ahmed Nacef, Mehdi Ben Ridha Ben Mohamed Ben Gaied, Ghazoua Bent Zine El Abidine Ben Haj Hamda Ben Ali), 24 listed persons with updated judicial information
- **shift_type**: restricted
- **shift_summary**: Removes three individuals from Tunisia sanctions list while extending general measures to 2027 and updating judicial procedure information for 24 persons

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Critical infrastructure (financial system sanctions enforcement)
- Financial compliance (asset freeze compliance)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Extend restrictive measures from 31 January 2026 to 31 January 2027
  - Remove asset freezes for three delisted individuals effective immediately
  - Update internal records with judicial procedure information for 24 persons regarding rights of defence and judicial protection under Tunisian law
  - Maintain asset freezes and travel restrictions for remaining listed individuals
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated sanctions lists reflecting delistings
  - Unfreezing documentation for three removed individuals
  - Records of judicial information updates for 24 affected persons
  - Continued monitoring records for remaining sanctioned entities
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
此決定延長突尼西亞制裁措施至 2027 年 1 月 31 日，同時將三人從制裁名單中移除，並更新 24 人的司法程序資訊（突尼西亞法律下的辯護權和司法保護）。所有成員國金融機構必須立即更新內部制裁清單並解除對三名除名個人的資產凍結。
