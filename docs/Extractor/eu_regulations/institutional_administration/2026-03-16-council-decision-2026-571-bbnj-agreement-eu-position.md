## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: The Council establishes the EU's formal negotiating position for the first Conference of the Parties to the BBNJ Agreement under UNCLOS, committing to efficient, transparent rules of procedure and equitable financial contribution mechanisms.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU and member state representatives must vote and act in accordance with the position defined in this decision at the BBNJ Preparatory Commission and first Conference of the Parties
  - Support rules of procedure ensuring efficient, cost-effective, transparent and orderly functioning
  - Support financial rules establishing a fair and transparent contribution process with assistance mechanisms for developing States
