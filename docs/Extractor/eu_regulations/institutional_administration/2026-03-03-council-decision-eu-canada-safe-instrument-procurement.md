## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:

  - Canadian legal entities gain access to EU SAFE Instrument procurement processes under defined conditions
  - Both parties must notify each other upon completion of internal procedures for provisional application
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Notification to counterpart confirming completion of required internal procedures
  - Compliance with conditions of the Agreement including product origin verification
