## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Transport Community Permanent Secretariat staff members, EU representatives in Regional Steering Committee, Regional Steering Committee
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Establishes EU negotiating position to extend maternity leave entitlements from unspecified to 20-24 weeks with full pay for Transport Community staff

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Employment rights
- International cooperation governance
- Regional institutional administration

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU representatives must support amending Transport Community Staff Regulations to provide 20 weeks maternity leave (24 weeks for complications/multiple births)
  - Maternity leave requires presentation of medical certificate
  - Full pay maintained during maternity leave
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Medical certificate for maternity leave
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This decision establishes the EU's negotiating position within the Transport Community's Regional Steering Committee, rather than creating direct obligations for EU Member States or businesses. The Transport Community is a regional cooperation framework for Southeast Europe. Category assigned as institutional_administration due to focus on internal governance of an EU-associated regional body.
