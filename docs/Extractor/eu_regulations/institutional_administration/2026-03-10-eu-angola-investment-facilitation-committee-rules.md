- **new_obligations**:

  - Decision-making to proceed by consensus
  - English and Portuguese designated as working languages
  - Public access to agendas and approved meeting minutes
  - Each party bears its own participation costs; host party covers meeting organisation and interpretation
- **evidence_requirements**:
