## L4: 操作衝擊

### new_obligations

**For EU Member States**:
- Automatic asset freezes on listed entities
- Transaction prohibition enforcement
- Export control verification for dual-use items
- Enhanced supply chain monitoring

**For Financial Institutions**:
- Screen all transactions against updated sanctions list
- Freeze assets of listed persons and entities
- Report sanctions evasion attempts
- Enhanced due diligence on Iran-related transactions

**For Export Authorities**:
- Deny export licenses for goods to listed entities
- Monitor potential sanctions circumvention schemes
- Coordinate with other Member States on enforcement

### evidence_requirements

- Corporate ownership and control documentation
- Supply chain traceability records
- Transaction monitoring logs
- Export license application screening records

## Notes

**Rationale**: Targets Iranian entities and individuals supporting Russia's war effort in Ukraine and armed groups in the Middle East through provision of UAVs, missiles, electronic components, and logistics support.

**Legal Basis**: Article 29, Treaty on European Union

**Related Legislation**: Decision (CFSP) 2023/1532 concerning restrictive measures in view of Iran's military support to Russia

**Context**: Part of broader EU sanctions strategy addressing Iran's role in supplying military equipment to Russia for use in Ukraine conflict and to armed groups in Middle East/Red Sea region.
