## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: European Union and Member States, Ukraine, European Committee of the Regions, Ukrainian elected local and regional authorities
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Establishes institutional mechanisms for EU-Ukraine dialogue on public administration reform and local/regional governance, creating a Public Administration Reform Sub-Committee and endorsing a Joint Consultative Committee under the EU-Ukraine Association Agreement.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- EU-Ukraine institutional cooperation and governance
- Public administration reform in countries seeking EU integration
- Regional and local authority engagement in candidate countries

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - The Public Administration Reform Sub-Committee shall serve as a platform for strategic policy dialogue and monitor implementation of public administration reform roadmaps
  - The Joint Consultative Committee shall comprise equal representation from the European Committee of the Regions and Ukrainian local/regional elected officials
  - Expenditure for Joint Consultative Committee meetings is borne by the European Committee of the Regions and Ukrainian government
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - No specific enforcement mechanisms; operates through cooperative institutional framework
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
