## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **shift_summary**: The Council mandates EU member states with CND seats to express a unified EU position supporting scheduling of three new psychoactive substances at the Commission on Narcotic Drugs 69th session, with direct implications for EU drug control law.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - 16 designated EU member states must vote jointly to support scheduling of: N-pyrrolidino isotonitazene (Schedule I, Narcotic Drugs Convention); N-desethyl etonitazene (Schedule I); MDMB-FUBINACA (Schedule II, Psychotropic Substances Convention)
  - Scheduled substances to be incorporated into EU common drug control rules under Framework Decision 2004/757/JHA
  - Member states must align national drug control frameworks upon scheduling adoption
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - WHO expert committee recommendations form the basis for scheduling proposals
  - Impact on scope of EU law under Framework Decision 2004/757/JHA documented
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
