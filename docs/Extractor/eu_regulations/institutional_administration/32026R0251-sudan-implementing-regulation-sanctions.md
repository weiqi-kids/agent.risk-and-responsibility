# Council Implementing Regulation (EU) 2026/251 - Sudan Sanctions Implementation

## 基本資訊

**CELEX 編號**: 32026R0251

**官方公報**: OJ L, 2026/251, 29.1.2026

**通過日期**: 2026-01-29

**生效日期**: Upon publication

**法規類型**: **regulation**

**約束力等級**: **directly_applicable**

**法律基礎**: Article 13(1) of Regulation (EU) 2023/2147 concerning restrictive measures for Sudan stability

## 規範主題

Implements Regulation (EU) 2023/2147 by amending sanctions list to add seven individuals involved in Rapid Support Forces (RSF) and Sudanese Armed Forces (SAF) activities that violate human rights and international humanitarian law.

## 責任轉移態樣

**轉移類型**: **expanded**

**執行信號**: **mandatory**

**受規範對象**:
- Seven Sudanese military commanders and affiliated individuals
- EU Member States (automatic enforcement)
- EU financial institutions and economic operators

**新增責任內容**:

1. **Designated Persons** (Seven individuals):

   **RSF-affiliated**:
   - RSF major involved in arms procurement
   - RSF commanders identified as perpetrators of El Fasher atrocities (October 2025)

   **SAF-affiliated**:
   - Militia commander responsible for summary executions in Khartoum and Gezirah
   - Community leader assisting SAF recruitment campaigns targeting specific ethnic groups

2. **Enforcement Obligations** (Immediate and automatic):
   - **Asset freezes**: All funds and economic resources belonging to listed persons frozen
   - **Transaction prohibitions**: Making funds/resources available to listed persons prohibited
   - **Due diligence**: Financial institutions must screen transactions against updated Annex I
   - **Reporting**: Attempted violations must be reported to competent authorities

3. **Automatic Application**: Regulation is "binding in its entirety and directly applicable in all Member States" - no national implementing legislation required

## 實作時程與檢查點

**Immediate Effect**: Regulation entered into force upon publication (29 January 2026)

**Compliance Timeline**:
- **T+0** (Publication date): Asset freezes take effect
- **T+0**: Financial institutions must screen all transactions
- **Ongoing**: Member States enforce prohibitions through national competent authorities

**No Transition Period**: Directly applicable regulation requires immediate compliance

## 風險與注意事項

**Coordination with CFSP Decision**:
- This regulation **implements** Council Decision (CFSP) 2026/254 (same seven individuals)
- Decision provides political/strategic basis; Regulation provides legal enforcement mechanism
- Both instruments entered into force same date (29 January 2026)

**Enforcement Mechanisms**:
- Asset freezes administered by national competent authorities
- Criminal penalties for violations per Member State national law
- Financial institutions face sanctions for non-compliance

**Compliance Challenges**:
- Arms procurement networks may use front companies or intermediaries
- RSF commanders may hold assets through family members or business structures
- SAF recruitment funding may flow through community organizations

**Accountability Framework**:
- Listings reference specific atrocities (El Fasher October 2025)
- Documentation from UN Human Rights Council and Human Rights Watch
- Geographic specificity (Khartoum North, Gezirah, El Fasher, North Darfur)

## 與其他法規的關聯

- **Regulation (EU) 2023/2147**: Primary Sudan sanctions regulation (amended by this instrument)
- **Council Decision (CFSP) 2026/254**: CFSP decision listing same seven individuals (political basis)
- **Regulation (EU) 2023/1525**: Asset freeze implementation procedures
- **Member State Criminal Law**: Penalties for sanctions violations
