## L1 — Rule Signal
- **rule_type**: amendment

- **document_id**: CELEX:32026R0087
- **status**: final
- **document_type**: regulation
- **binding_force**: directly_applicable
- **effective_date**: 2026-01-13 (day following publication)

## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: All EU Member States, Guatemalan officials and entities subject to sanctions
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Operationalizes EU restrictive measures framework by updating sanctions list to reflect current threats to democratic governance in Guatemala

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Geopolitical risk (sanctions enforcement)
- Governance accountability (judicial independence)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Implementation of amended Annex I to Regulation (EU) 2024/287
  - Direct applicability across all Member States without requiring transposition
  - Asset freezes and travel restrictions against five listed individuals
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Evidence of spurious criminal investigations targeting election authorities
  - Evidence of politically-motivated prosecutions against media and civil leaders
  - Evidence of state capture and undermining rule of law
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This implementing regulation is binding and directly applicable in all Member States. The five individuals listed are Attorney General María Consuelo Porras Argueta De Porres, Secretary-General Ángel Arnoldo Pineda Ávila, FECI Head José Rafael Curruchiche Cucul, and Foundation Against Terrorism representatives Ricardo Rafael Méndez-Ruiz Valdés and Raúl Amílcar Falla Ovalle.
