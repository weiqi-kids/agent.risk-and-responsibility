## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Transport Community Permanent Secretariat, EU Council representatives
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Establishes EU position for amending Transport Community Permanent Secretariat maternity leave provisions to 20 weeks with full pay (24 weeks under specified circumstances)

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Institutional administration
- Personnel policy

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU representatives to support maternity leave amendment in Regional Steering Committee
  - Grant pregnant employees 20 weeks maternity leave with full pay
  - Extend to 24 weeks for caesarean sections or multiple births
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Regional Steering Committee approval documentation
  - Staff Regulations amendment records
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Scope limited to Transport Community Permanent Secretariat internal administration. Does not directly impact member states or commercial entities.
