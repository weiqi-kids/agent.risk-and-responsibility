## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Nine listed Russian military officers, EU member states' competent authorities, financial institutions and service providers in EU jurisdiction
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: The EU directly applies asset freezes and travel bans to nine Russian military personnel by adding them to Annex I of Regulation (EU) No 269/2014, based on alleged involvement in war crimes and crimes against humanity in Ukraine.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- EU sanctions compliance, financial institutions' restrictive measures obligations

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Financial institutions and relevant service providers must immediately freeze assets of the nine newly listed individuals
  - Competent authorities must enforce travel ban and asset freeze measures across all EU member states
  - Entities must screen against updated Annex I of Regulation (EU) No 269/2014
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated Annex I with nine individuals' identifying information and listing justifications
  - Documentation of alleged involvement in atrocities (executions, torture, looting, civilian casualties in Bucha and other locations)
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
