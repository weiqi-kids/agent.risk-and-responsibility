## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU member states, financial institutions, media organisations, EU persons and entities with links to designated propaganda actors
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: The EU expands Russia destabilisation sanctions by adding four individuals engaged in disinformation and propaganda supporting Russia's war against Ukraine.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Institutional administration (EU sanctions implementation against foreign disinformation actors)
- Critical infrastructure resilience (information environment, democratic processes)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Asset freeze for four individuals: Sergey Klyuchenkov, Ernest Mackevicius, Graham Williams Phillips, Adrien Bocquet
  - Prohibition on making funds or economic resources available to designated persons
  - Member states must enforce immediately upon publication
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Each designation supported by documented evidence of spreading propaganda or disinformation justifying Russia's military actions
  - Mackevicius designated as leading anchor on Russian state television
  - Phillips designated as producing content from Russian-occupied Ukrainian territories
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
