## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU Member States, nine listed Russian military personnel (including Colonel General Aleksandr Chayko and Major General Sergey Chubarykin), financial institutions and border authorities responsible for enforcement
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Implements asset freeze and travel ban measures against nine Russian military officers by amending Annex I to Regulation (EU) No 269/2014, complementing Council Decision (CFSP) 2026/614 on Ukraine-related sanctions.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - All EU Member State financial institutions must freeze assets of the nine listed individuals immediately upon publication
  - Border authorities must enforce travel bans against listed persons
  - Annex I to Regulation (EU) No 269/2014 amended to include nine individuals from 76th Guards Air Assault Division and related units
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Legal basis: Article 14(1) of Council Regulation (EU) No 269/2014
  - Individuals listed for alleged involvement in Bucha and Kyiv Oblast atrocities (Feb–Apr 2022)
  - Directly applicable regulation; no transposition required
