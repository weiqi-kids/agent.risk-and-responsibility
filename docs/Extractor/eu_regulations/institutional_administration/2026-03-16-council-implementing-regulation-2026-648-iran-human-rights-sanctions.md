## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU member states, financial institutions, EU operators, persons dealing with listed Iranian officials and entities
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: The EU adds 16 Iranian individuals and 3 entities to the restrictive measures list under Regulation (EU) No 359/2011 in response to serious human rights violations during December 2025 protests.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Institutional administration (EU sanctions enforcement, human rights conditionality)
- Supply chain (Iranian IT services firm designated)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Asset freeze for 16 individuals including IRGC commanders, police chiefs, prison administrators, prosecutors, and judges
  - Asset freeze for 3 entities: Naji Research and Development Company, Mohammad Rasulullah Corps, Imam Reza IRGC provincial branch
  - Travel ban for listed individuals
  - EU persons must not make funds or economic resources available to listed parties
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Designations based on documented involvement in suppression of protests, arbitrary detention, torture, and denial of fair trial rights
  - Specific evidence tied to events commencing December 2025
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
