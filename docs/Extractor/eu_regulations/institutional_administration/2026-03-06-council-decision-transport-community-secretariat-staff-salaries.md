## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU must support Regional Steering Committee adoption of a 20% salary increase for all Secretariat staff effective 1 January 2026
  - Annual salary indexation to commence 1 January 2027, based on Serbia's Consumer Price Index
  - Salary ranges: Assistants €2,400–€3,000/month, Director €9,600–€12,000/month
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Decision taken under Article 218(9) TFEU and pursuant to Article 30 of the Transport Community Treaty
  - EU position must be formally communicated to the Regional Steering Committee
