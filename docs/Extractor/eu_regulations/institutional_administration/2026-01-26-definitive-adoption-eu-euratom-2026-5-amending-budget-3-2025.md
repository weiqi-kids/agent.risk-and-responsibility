## L4: 操作衝擊

### new_obligations

**For EU Member States**:
- Provide adjusted own resource contributions based on revised calculations
- Update national budget execution to reflect increased EU contributions
- Process revised GNI-based, VAT-based, and plastic waste-based contributions
- Coordinate with national customs authorities on increased traditional own resources

**For National Finance Ministries**:
- Adjust treasury operations to transfer increased contributions to EU budget
- Reconcile national accounts with revised EU own resources calculations
- Ensure timely payment of adjusted contributions

**For EU Institutions**:
- Execute amended budget in accordance with revised appropriations
- Allocate additional revenue to designated budget lines
- Maintain compliance with Financial Regulation (EU, Euratom) 2024/2509

### evidence_requirements

**Member State Contribution Documentation**:
- Detailed breakdown tables specifying per-Member State contributions (Tables 1-4 in budget document)
- Traditional own resources calculations (customs duties, sugar levies)
- VAT-based own resources calculations
- Plastic waste-based own resources calculations
- GNI-based own resources calculations

**Budget Execution Records**:
- Revenue collection documentation by source
- Expenditure authorization and payment records
- Compliance reports under Financial Regulation

### enforcement_signal

## Notes

**Purpose**: Amends EU budget for financial year 2025 to reflect increased revenue from customs duties, competition fines, and member state penalties.

**Legal Basis**: Article 314 TFEU (budgetary procedure)

**Parliamentary Approval**: 27 November 2025

**Total Revenue Impact**: EUR 2,438,206,133 increase

**Major Revenue Sources**:
- Customs duties (traditional own resources)
- Competition fines (from enforcement actions)
- Member State penalty payments (infringement procedures)

**Document Status**: Historical record—budget year 2025 concluded on 31 December 2025.

**Related Legislation**: Financial Regulation (EU, Euratom) 2024/2509

**Context**: Reflects mid-year budget adjustments based on higher-than-projected customs revenue, significant competition law enforcement fines, and member state penalty payments for EU law violations.
