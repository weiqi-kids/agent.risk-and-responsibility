  - Evidence of efforts to overturn 2023 election results

## Notes
This decision targets specific individuals accused of violations of democracy, rule of law, and peaceful power transfer principles in Guatemala. The updated annex lists Attorney General María Consuelo Porras Argueta, Secretary-General Ángel Pineda Ávila, FECI Head José Rafael Curruchiche Cucul, and Foundation against Terrorism leaders Ricardo Méndez-Ruiz Valdés and Raúl Falla Ovalle.
