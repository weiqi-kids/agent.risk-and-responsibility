## L4: 操作衝擊

### new_obligations

**For European Commission**:
- Conduct negotiations on behalf of the Union for Australia participation agreement
- Follow Council directives contained in decision addendum
- Consult with Council working parties (Asia-Oceania and Research) on participation conditions
- Report negotiation progress to Council
- Finalize agreement terms within negotiation mandate

**For Council Working Parties**:
- Provide consultation on programme participation conditions
- Review negotiation progress and draft agreement terms
- Advise on alignment with EU research and innovation objectives

**For Future Agreement (once concluded)**:
- Australian entities may participate in Horizon Europe Pillar II activities
- Terms and conditions to be specified in final agreement
- Financial contributions and participation rights to be defined

## Notes

**Purpose**: Establishes legal authority for EU to negotiate agreement enabling Australia's participation in Horizon Europe research and innovation programmes, strengthening EU-Australia scientific cooperation.

**Legal Basis**: Articles 186, 212, and 218(3)-(4) of the Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union

**Programme Context**: Horizon Europe is the EU's key funding programme for research and innovation, operating from 2021-2027 with a budget of approximately €95.5 billion.

**Third-Country Participation**: Australia would join other associated countries participating in Horizon Europe, subject to terms negotiated and agreed.

**No Direct Obligations**: This decision does not establish direct obligations for member states or stakeholders—it initiates a negotiation process for potential future agreement terms that will be defined through negotiations.
