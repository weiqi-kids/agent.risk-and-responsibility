# Decision of the Bureau of the European Parliament amending the Implementing Measures for the Statute for Members of the European Parliament

## Metadata
- **CELEX Number**: 32026D00685
- **Document Type**: decision
- **Binding Force**: directly_applicable
- **Date of Adoption**: 2026-01-19
- **Date of Entry into Force**: 2026-01-31 (day following publication)
- **Date of Application**: 2026-02-01
- **Publication Reference**: OJ C, 2026/685, 30.1.2026
- **ELI**: http://data.europa.eu/eli/C/2026/685/oj
- **Legal Basis**: TFEU Article 223(2); European Parliament Rules of Procedure Rule 25
- **Amends**: Implementing Measures for the Statute for Members of the European Parliament

## Objective
Annual adjustment of Members of the European Parliament (MEPs) compensation provisions using October 2025 EU inflation rate (2.5%) to maintain real value of reimbursements and allowances.

## Key Provisions

### Inflation Indexation Basis
All adjustments calculated using **October 2025 EU inflation rate of 2.5%**.

### Specific Compensation Adjustments

#### 1. Health Insurance Waiver Reimbursement
- **Previous**: EUR 400 per month
- **Adjusted**: **EUR 410 per month**
- **Applicability**: MEPs waiving medical expense coverage

#### 2. Travel Reimbursement Rate
- **Previous**: EUR 0.57/km
- **Adjusted**: **EUR 0.59/km**
- **Applicability**: Ground transportation expenses

#### 3. Daily Subsistence Allowance (0-50km distance)
- **Previous**: Not specified in excerpt
- **Adjusted**: **EUR 26.56** per journey segment
- **Applicability**: Travel within 0-50km radius

#### 4. Annual Travel Ceiling (Standard Members)
- **Previous**: Not specified in excerpt
- **Adjusted**: **EUR 5,638**
- **Applicability**: Total annual travel reimbursement cap for standard MEPs

#### 5. Annual Travel Ceiling (Committee/Subcommittee Chairs)
- **Previous**: Not specified in excerpt
- **Adjusted**: **EUR 5,008**
- **Applicability**: Conference travel reimbursement cap for chairs

#### 6. Meeting Attendance Allowance
- **Previous**: Not specified in excerpt
- **Adjusted**: **EUR 359** per session
- **Applicability**: Domestic meeting attendance

## Risk & Responsibility Impact

### Shift Type
- clarified

### Responsibility Changes

1. **Bureau of the European Parliament**:
   - Approve annual inflation adjustments based on official EU inflation data
   - Ensure indexation does not exceed annual inflation rate
   - Maintain transparency on compensation adjustments

2. **MEPs (705 members)**:
   - Comply with updated reimbursement ceilings
   - Submit expense claims under revised rates
   - Maintain documentation for travel and attendance allowances

3. **European Parliament Administration (DG FINS)**:
   - Implement updated reimbursement rates in financial systems
   - Process claims under new ceilings from 2026-02-01
   - Audit compliance with annual caps
   - Publish transparency data on MEP expenses

4. **Internal Auditor**:
   - Verify appropriateness of inflation adjustment methodology
   - Audit expense claims for compliance with ceilings

### Enforcement Signal
- mandatory

### Sectors Affected
- European Parliament administration
- MEP office management
- Parliamentary financial services
- Travel service providers

### Jurisdictional Applicability
**Member States Bound**: Indirectly applicable (MEPs from all Member States subject to EU Parliament rules)
**EEA Relevance**: No (applies only to EU institution)
**Third Country Effect**: No direct third-country effect

## Key Obligations

### MEP Obligations
1. Claim reimbursements within updated ceilings
2. Maintain receipts and documentation for expense claims
3. Comply with annual travel caps (EUR 5,638 standard; EUR 5,008 chairs)
4. Submit claims through official expense management system

### Parliament Administration Obligations
1. Update financial management systems by 2026-02-01
2. Process claims under new rates
3. Conduct audits to ensure compliance with ceilings
4. Publish annual transparency reports on MEP expenses

### Bureau Obligations
1. Review inflation adjustments annually
2. Ensure adjustments do not exceed inflation rate
3. Maintain Statute implementing measures in line with TFEU Article 223(2)

## Implementation Requirements

### Timeline
- **Adoption**: 2026-01-19
- **Entry into Force**: 2026-01-31 (day following publication)
- **Application Date**: 2026-02-01
- **Next Annual Review**: Expected January 2027 (based on October 2026 inflation)

### Implementation Measures Required
1. **Financial system updates**: Modify expense management software to reflect new rates and ceilings
2. **Communication**: Notify all MEPs of updated reimbursement provisions
3. **Training**: Update Parliament staff on new reimbursement processing procedures
4. **Audit adjustments**: Modify audit protocols to verify compliance with 2026 ceilings

## Compliance Challenges
1. **Transitional claims**: Ensuring expenses incurred in January 2026 are processed under correct rates (old vs. new)
2. **System updates**: Timely implementation of IT system changes by 2026-02-01
3. **MEP awareness**: Ensuring all 705 MEPs are informed of updated ceilings and rates
4. **Cross-border travel complexity**: Applying correct per-kilometer rates for international travel
5. **Audit capacity**: Verifying compliance with annual caps across 705 MEP offices

This decision continues the established practice of annual inflation indexation for MEP compensation, ensuring purchasing power parity across parliamentary terms and Member States while maintaining fiscal discipline through capped reimbursements.
