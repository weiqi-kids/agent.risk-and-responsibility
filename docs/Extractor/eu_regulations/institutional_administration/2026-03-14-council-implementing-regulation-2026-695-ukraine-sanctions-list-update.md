## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU member states, financial institutions, compliance officers, EU entities screening counterparties against sanctions lists
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Annex I to Regulation (EU) No 269/2014 is updated to replace entries for 132 individuals and 77 entities with revised identifying information, and to delete entries for five deceased persons and two others.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Institutional administration (EU sanctions list maintenance, Ukraine-related restrictive measures)
- Supply chain (sanctions screening obligations for EU businesses)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU persons and entities must use updated identifying information when performing sanctions screening for the 132 individuals and 77 entities whose entries are replaced
  - Asset freeze and prohibition on making funds available to listed parties remains in force
  - Entries for five deceased persons and two others deleted; no obligations apply to removed parties from date of entry into force
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated reasons for listing provided for each replaced entry
  - Documentation of individual-level involvement in actions threatening Ukraine's territorial integrity and sovereignty
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
