## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU Member States, competent national authorities, financial institutions, border control authorities
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Expands EU restrictive measures on Sudan by adding four RSF military commanders designated under UNSC Resolution 1591 for atrocities committed in El Fasher on 26 October 2025.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Sanctions compliance and enforcement
- International humanitarian law violations
- Geopolitical risk (Sudan/Darfur conflict)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Member States must freeze assets and financial resources of four newly listed individuals: Hamdan Ahmed Gedo, Abdul Rahim Hamdan Dagalo, Abdullah Idris Al-Fateh, and Tijani Ibrahim Moussa Mohamed
  - Travel bans and asset freeze apply immediately upon publication
  - Competent authorities must implement restrictions under the parent Regulation (EU) No 747/2014
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Designation based on UNSC Committee listings (established under Resolution 1591) of 24 February 2026
  - Documented acts: mass killings of civilians, ethnically targeted executions in El Fasher
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
