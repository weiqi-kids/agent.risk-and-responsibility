## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **shift_summary**: Appoints Mr Ciprian CIUCU (General Mayor of Bucharest Municipality) as alternate member of the Committee of the Regions, proposed by Romania, through 25 January 2030.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Mr Ciprian CIUCU appointed as alternate member of the Committee of the Regions for term running to 25 January 2030
  - Appointment is contingent on appointee holding "a regional or local authority electoral mandate or is politically accountable to an elected assembly"
  - Romania proposed the candidate following conclusion of the prior national mandate
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Mr CIUCU holds the position of General Mayor of Bucharest Municipality, satisfying the democratic mandate requirement
  - Romania submitted formal proposal to the Council
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
