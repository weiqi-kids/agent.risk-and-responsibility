## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Belgium (applying member state), Audi Brussels S.A. and five related companies, displaced workers and self-employed persons, European Commission (EGF managing authority)
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: EUR 7,527,625 is mobilised from the European Globalisation Adjustment Fund for Displaced Workers to support Belgian workers displaced by the restructuring and closure of Audi Brussels (case EGF/2025/006 BE/Audi).

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Labour market disruption and worker displacement
- Industrial restructuring and just transition
- EU budgetary commitments and social fund governance

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Belgium to deploy EGF funds to support re-employment of displaced workers and self-employed persons
  - Funds to be used for personalised services assisting workers to return to "decent and sustainable employment"
  - EUR 7,527,625 committed as 2026 budget appropriations (both commitment and payment)
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Belgium must document eligible displaced workers from Audi Brussels and related suppliers/downstream producers
  - Reporting on fund utilisation and re-employment outcomes to the European Commission
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
