## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: European Parliament Members, Parliament financial services, reimbursement administrators
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Implements 2.5% annual indexation adjustments to MEPs' allowances and reimbursement limits based on October 2025 inflation rate

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Institutional administration
- Financial management

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Apply health insurance reimbursement ceiling of EUR 410 per month
  - Apply travel reimbursement rate of EUR 0.59/km
  - Apply daily subsistence allowance of EUR 359
  - Cap annual travel expenses at EUR 5,638 (standard) or EUR 5,008 (committee chairs)
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Expense claim documentation
  - Reimbursement calculation records
  - Inflation index verification
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Scope limited to European Parliament internal administration. Annual indexation based on October 2025 inflation rate of 2.5%.
