## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU member states, Republic of Korea, audiovisual producers in EU and Korea engaged in joint co-productions
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Renews the EU-Korea audiovisual co-production entitlement period from 1 July 2026 through 30 June 2029, allowing joint productions to access domestic content promotion schemes in both jurisdictions.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Institutional administration (bilateral cultural cooperation agreement renewal)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU-Korea audiovisual co-productions may access domestic promotion schemes of each party from 1 July 2026 to 30 June 2029
  - Renewal based on committee assessment confirming the arrangement enhances cultural diversity and mutual cooperation
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Compliance with Article 5 of the Protocol on Cultural Cooperation to the EU-Korea Free Trade Agreement
  - Committee assessment confirming qualifying conditions are met
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
WebFetch succeeded. Administrative renewal of a bilateral cultural cooperation provision under the EU-Korea Free Trade Agreement. No substantive regulatory impact on member states' enterprises beyond the audiovisual production sector.
