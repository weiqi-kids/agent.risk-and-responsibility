# Council Decision (CFSP) 2026/232 - European Peace Facility Assistance to Armenia

## 基本資訊

**CELEX 編號**: 32026D0232

**官方公報**: OJ L, 2026/232, 30.1.2026

**通過日期**: 2026-01-29

**生效日期**: Upon publication

**法規類型**: **decision**

**約束力等級**: **directly_applicable**

**法律基礎**: TEU Article 28(1) and Article 41(2); Decision (CFSP) 2021/509 establishing European Peace Facility

## 規範主題

Establishes EUR 20 million assistance measure under the European Peace Facility to strengthen military and defence capacities of the Armed Forces of the Republic of Armenia through non-lethal equipment provision over 35 months.

## 責任轉移態樣

**轉移類型**: **new**

**執行信號**: **mandatory**

**受規範對象**:
- High Representative of the Union for Foreign Affairs and Security Policy (implementation authority)
- Republic of Armenia Armed Forces (beneficiary)
- Political and Security Committee (oversight body)

**新增責任內容**:

1. **Financial Commitment**: EUR 20 million allocation for 35-month implementation period

2. **Equipment Provision Scope**:
   - Non-lethal equipment focusing on logistics and medical capabilities
   - Scaling "battalion-size tent camp and role-1 medical treatment facility to brigade level"
   - Equipment must remain with identified recipient units only

3. **Beneficiary Obligations** (High Representative must secure arrangements requiring Armenia to ensure):
   - Compliance with "international human rights law and international humanitarian law" by supported units
   - "Proper and efficient use" of provided assets
   - Maintenance standards preserving "operational availability over their life cycle"
   - Assets remain exclusively with designated recipients

4. **Monitoring and Accountability**:
   - Annual inventory reporting on designated items
   - On-site verification visits and audits
   - Six-monthly implementation reports to Political and Security Committee
   - Final evaluation determining whether objectives were achieved

5. **Suspension Authority**: Political and Security Committee may suspend implementation for breaches of Article 3 obligations

## 實作時程與檢查點

**Implementation Period**: 35 months from decision entry into force

**Reporting Requirements**:
- Six-monthly implementation reports to PSC
- Annual inventory reporting on designated items
- On-site verification visits (frequency not specified)
- Final evaluation upon completion

**Decision Authority**: Political and Security Committee exercises oversight and suspension powers

## 風險與注意事項

**Compliance Risks**:
- Breach of IHL/IHRL by supported units may trigger PSC suspension
- Improper use or maintenance of assets may result in suspension
- Unauthorized transfer of assets to non-designated recipients

**Accountability Mechanisms**:
- Inventory tracking system for all designated items
- On-site verification and audit authority
- Regular reporting cycles to PSC
- Final evaluation assessing objective achievement

**Geopolitical Context**: Decision aims to "enhance national security and support EU cooperation frameworks" with Armenia, implicitly addressing regional security dynamics in South Caucasus.
