- **affected_roles**: All EU Member States (contracting States to the Chicago Convention), ICAO Council, European Network of Civil Aviation Safety Investigation Authorities (ENCASIA)

- **shift_summary**: Establishes the EU's coordinated position to support adoption of Amendment 20 to Annex 13 (Aircraft Accident and Incident Investigation) at the ICAO Council's 237th session, requiring Member States to act jointly.

- Civil aviation safety investigation standards

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - The Union shall support proposed Amendment 20 at the ICAO Council session
  - Member States shall not register disapproval once adopted unless substantial changes occur
  - The Commission must submit preparatory documents at least two months before ICAO deadlines if EU legislation differs from the new standards
  - Member States must act jointly to express the Union's position
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Implementation through coordinated Member State action in ICAO proceedings
  - Compliance notifications via ICAO State Letter procedure
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
