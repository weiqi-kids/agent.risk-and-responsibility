## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU representatives must support adoption of the Committee's Rules of Procedure as described
  - Rules include: committee co-chairs from each party, 14-day advance notice for agenda items, decision-making by consensus, written procedure option between meetings
  - Committee decisions on Rules of Procedure will be binding on the Union once adopted
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Formal adoption of Rules of Procedure at Committee level
  - Minutes and authenticated decisions to be maintained per Rules
