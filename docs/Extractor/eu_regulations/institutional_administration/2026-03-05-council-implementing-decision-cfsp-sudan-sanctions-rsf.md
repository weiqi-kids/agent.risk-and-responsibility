## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU Member States, designated individuals (RSF commanders), competent national authorities, financial institutions
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Four RSF commanders in Sudan are added to the EU CFSP restrictive measures list under Decision 2014/450/CFSP, expanding designations to cover perpetrators of mass killings and sexual violence in El Fasher, North Darfur.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Member States must enforce asset freezes and travel bans against four newly listed individuals
  - Competent authorities must implement updated Annex to Decision 2014/450/CFSP
  - Listing aligns with UN Security Council designations of 24 February 2026
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Compliance with updated Annex to Decision 2014/450/CFSP
  - Records of measures taken to restrict travel and freeze assets of listed persons
