## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Union authorised to sign the Partnership Agreement subject to formal conclusion
  - Specified chapters apply provisionally pending entry into force (excluding consular protection, tax matters, and other reserved provisions)
  - Both parties must provide required notifications for provisional application
  - Provisional application date to be published in the Official Journal
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Completion of internal procedures by both EU and Mercosur parties before provisional application begins
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
