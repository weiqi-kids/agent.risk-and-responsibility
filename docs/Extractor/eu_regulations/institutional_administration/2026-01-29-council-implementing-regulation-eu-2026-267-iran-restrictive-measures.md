## L2: 權責轉移分析

**affected_roles**:
- EU Member States (asset freeze enforcement)
- Financial institutions (sanctions compliance)
- Listed individuals (15 Iranian government/security officials)
- Listed entities (6 Iranian organizations)

**shift_type**: Authority → Enforcement

**shift_summary**: Regulation amends Annex I to Regulation (EU) No 359/2011 by adding 15 natural persons and 6 entities to the sanctions list targeting human rights violations in Iran. Member States must implement asset freezes and transaction restrictions immediately.

---

## L3: Risk Domains

**Primary Domains**:
- Human rights violations
- Internet censorship and surveillance
- Government oppression and suppression of dissent
- Financial sanctions compliance

**Listed Individuals Include**:
- Minister of Interior
- Prosecutor General
- IRGC commanders
- Revolutionary Court judges
- Cyberspace governance officials

**Listed Entities Include**:
- Audio-Visual Media Regulatory Authority (SATRA)
- Seraj Cyberspace Organization
- Working Group for Determining Instances of Criminal Content
- Internet censorship technology firms (Yaftar, Douran Software Technologies)
- Masaf Institute (disinformation operations)

---

## L4: 操作衝擊

### new_obligations

**For EU Member States**:
- Immediate enforcement of asset freezes on listed persons and entities
- Transaction prohibition implementation
- Sanctions compliance monitoring

**For Financial Institutions**:
- Screen transactions against updated sanctions list
- Report attempted violations to competent authorities
- Implement enhanced due diligence procedures

### evidence_requirements

- Identity verification documentation for listed individuals/entities
- Transaction screening records
- Compliance audit trails

### enforcement_signal

**Enforcement Mechanism**: Implementation follows existing Regulation (EU) No 359/2011 procedures for asset freezes and restrictive measures.

**Enforcement Authority**: Member State competent authorities

**Penalties**: As specified under Regulation (EU) No 359/2011 framework

**Compliance Timeline**: Immediate (upon entry into force on 2026-01-29)

---

## Notes

**Rationale**: Targets actors responsible for arbitrary detention, torture, violent protest suppression, internet censorship, surveillance infrastructure, and disinformation campaigns violating freedom of expression, assembly, and information access.

**Legal Basis**: Council Regulation (EU) No 359/2011

**Related Legislation**: EU sanctions framework concerning human rights violations
