## Level 2: Strategic Shift Summary

**Affected Roles**: Member state authorities responsible for sanctions enforcement, financial institutions monitoring restricted persons, entities conducting due diligence on Hamas-related financing

**Shift Type**: Administrative update (maintenance)

**Shift Summary**: Updates sanctions list by removing deceased individual (Ismail Barhoum) and updating entry for Sudan-based Hamas financier Abdelbasit Elhassan Mohamed Khair Hamza managing investment portfolio companies. Maintains restrictive measures framework without substantive policy change.

---

## Level 3: Risk Domains

- **Compliance & Regulatory**: Financial institutions must update sanctions screening systems to reflect revised list entries
- **Operational**: Entities conducting business in MENA region must verify counterparties against updated Hamas financing network information

---

## Level 4: Obligation, Evidence & Enforcement

### New Obligations
- Member States must apply restrictive measures (asset freezes, transaction prohibitions) against updated list of designated persons and entities
- Financial institutions must screen updated entry for Abdelbasit Elhassan Mohamed Khair Hamza (Sudan-based financier managing Hamas investment companies)
- Removal of deceased individual (Ismail Barhoum) from sanctions database

### Evidence Requirements
- Updated identifying information for sanctioned persons (name, date of birth, nationality, passport details where available)
- Documentation linking individuals to Hamas financing or facilitation activities
- Records of investment portfolio companies managed by designated financiers

### Enforcement Signal
- **Mandatory enforcement**: Direct applicability in all member states without transposition requirement
- **Compliance monitoring**: Member states report implementation through parent regulation framework (EU 2024/386)
- **Penalties**: Violations subject to national sanctions enforcement penalties under existing restrictive measures legislation

---

## Notes

This is a routine maintenance update to the Hamas sanctions list, reflecting operational intelligence (death of sanctioned individual) and updated information on active financiers. Organizations with MENA exposure should update compliance screening systems to reflect the revised Annex I entries, particularly the updated Sudan-based financing network details.
