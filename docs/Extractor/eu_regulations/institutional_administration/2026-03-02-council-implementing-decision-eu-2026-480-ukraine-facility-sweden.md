## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Sweden (contributing member state), European Commission (managing authority), Ukraine (beneficiary)
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Sweden commits an additional SEK 2,000,000,000 (approximately €180M) to Pillar I of the Ukraine Facility, expanding the financial support envelope managed by the European Commission under Regulation (EU) 2024/792.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- EU financial assistance mechanisms
- Geopolitical and macrofinancial risk

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Sweden must transfer SEK 2,000,000,000 as non-repayable support to the Ukraine Facility
  - European Commission must manage the contribution per EU financial procedures and allocate to the eighth quarterly instalment of the Ukraine Plan
  - Funds conditional on entry into force of a transfer agreement with Ukraine
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Execution of transfer agreement between Sweden and the Commission
  - Confirmation of fund transfer for allocation to Ukraine
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
WebFetch successful. Amends Implementing Decision (EU) 2024/1447. Funds are allocated to Ukraine's eighth quarterly installment under the Ukraine Plan. This decision reflects ongoing EU financial solidarity measures with Ukraine and has no direct compliance obligations for private sector entities.
