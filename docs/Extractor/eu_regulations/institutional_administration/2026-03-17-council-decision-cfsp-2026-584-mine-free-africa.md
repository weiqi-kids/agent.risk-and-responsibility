## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **shift_summary**: The EU commits EUR 3,000,000 to build mine clearance capacity in up to five African states under the Anti-Personnel Mine Ban Convention framework, implemented via UNIDIR and Mines Advisory Group.

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:

  - Support national mine clearance capacity building and knowledge exchange
  - Report on implementation progress to the Council
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Implementation agreement with UNIDIR
  - Progress reports on mine clearance activities in target states
