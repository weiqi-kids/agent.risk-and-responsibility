# Council Implementing Regulation (EU) 2026/238 - Tunisia Restrictive Measures

## 基本資訊

**CELEX 編號**: 32026R0238

**官方公報**: OJ L, 2026/238, 30.1.2026

**通過日期**: 2026-01-29

**生效日期**: Day following publication

**法規類型**: **regulation**

**約束力等級**: **directly_applicable**

**法律基礎**: TFEU; Council Regulation (EU) No 101/2011

## 規範主題

Amends Annex I to Regulation (EU) No 101/2011 by removing three individuals from Tunisia sanctions list and updating procedural information for 24 persons regarding rights of defence and judicial protection under Tunisian law.

## 責任轉移態樣

**轉移類型**: **restricted**

**執行信號**: **mandatory**

**受規範對象**:
- EU Member States (enforcement authorities)
- Financial institutions operating in EU
- Previously listed individuals (three persons removed from restrictions)

**責任變更內容**:

1. **Sanctions List Deletions** (Three individuals removed):
   - Lilia Bent Noureddine Ben Ahmed NACEF
   - Mehdi Ben Ridha Ben Mohamed BEN GAIED
   - Ghazoua Bent Zine El Abidine Ben Haj Hamda BEN ALI

2. **Procedural Information Updates**:
   - Information updated for 24 persons concerning "rights of defence and right to effective judicial protection"
   - Documents investigative procedures, legal representation, and judicial outcomes under Tunisian law
   - Reflects Council review process of individual cases

3. **Enforcement Changes**:
   - Asset freezes and transaction restrictions removed for three delisted individuals
   - Member States must cease enforcement measures against delisted persons
   - Updated procedural information provides transparency on due process compliance

## 實作時程與檢查點

**Immediate Effect**: Regulation entered into force the day following publication (31 January 2026)

**Member State Obligations**:
- Immediate cessation of restrictive measures against three delisted individuals
- Update internal enforcement databases with amended Annex I
- Notify affected financial institutions of changes

**No Phase-In Period**: Directly applicable regulation requires immediate compliance

## 風險與注意事項

**Compliance Requirements**:
- Financial institutions must promptly unfreeze assets of delisted individuals
- Transaction restrictions must be lifted immediately for removed persons
- Procedural information updates do not affect sanctions status of 24 updated entries (they remain listed)

**Accountability Mechanisms**:
- Regulation reflects Council's periodic review of individual listings
- Documented procedural safeguards include:
  - Legal counsel appointment
  - Investigative judge hearings
  - Appeal processes throughout Tunisian judicial proceedings

**Due Process Emphasis**: Updates demonstrate EU commitment to ensuring listed persons have access to effective judicial protection and rights of defence

## 與其他法規的關聯

- **Regulation (EU) No 101/2011**: Primary Tunisia sanctions framework (amended by this regulation)
- **TFEU**: Constitutional basis for restrictive measures
- **EU Charter of Fundamental Rights**: Right to effective judicial protection (implicit reference)
