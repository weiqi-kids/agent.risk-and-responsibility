# Council Decision (CFSP) 2026/254 - Sudan Restrictive Measures

## 基本資訊

**CELEX 編號**: 32026D0254

**官方公報**: OJ L, 2026/254, 29.1.2026

**通過日期**: 2026-01-29

**生效日期**: Upon publication

**法規類型**: **decision**

**約束力等級**: **directly_applicable**

**法律基礎**: TEU Article 29

## 規範主題

Amends Decision (CFSP) 2023/2135 by adding seven individuals to Sudan sanctions list for serious human rights violations and atrocities, particularly following RSF seizure of El Fasher in October 2025.

## 責任轉移態樣

**轉移類型**: **expanded**

**執行信號**: **mandatory**

**受規範對象**:
- Seven Sudanese military and militia commanders (natural persons)
- EU Member States (enforcement authorities)
- Financial institutions operating in EU

**新增責任內容**:

1. **Designated Rapid Support Forces (RSF) Commanders** (Five individuals):

   - **Algoney Hamdan Dagalo Musa**: RSF major, brother of RSF leader; arms procurement role; established companies supporting RSF procurement processes

   - **Elfateh Abdullah Idris Adam**: RSF Brigadier General, Commander; perpetrator of El Fasher atrocities including "civilian executions and killings of children"

   - **Edris Kafuti**: RSF field commander; perpetrator in El Fasher atrocities involving "detention harassment"

   - **Tijani Ibrahim Moussa Mohamed**: RSF field commander; perpetrator in El Fasher atrocities

   - **Gedo Hamdan**: RSF Regional Commander for North Darfur; "key perpetrator of El Fasher atrocities"

2. **Designated Sudanese Armed Forces (SAF) Allied Figures** (Two individuals):

   - **Abu Zaid Talha Al-Misbah**: Commander, Baraa bin Malik Battalion (allied militia); responsibility for "summary executions in Khartoum North and Gezirah"

   - **Al-Tayyib Al-Imam Joda**: Clan leader assisting SAF recruitment; "incited violence against civilian populations"; supported paramilitaries committing human rights violations

3. **Accountability Basis**:
   - Listed for "serious human rights violations or abuses or violations of international humanitarian law"
   - Threatening Sudan's "peace, stability, and security"
   - Documentation references December 2025 UN HRC Fact-Finding Mission and February 2025 Human Rights Watch findings

4. **Enforcement Obligations**:
   - Asset freezes for all seven individuals
   - Travel bans to EU territory
   - Prohibition on making funds/economic resources available
   - Due diligence for entities with Sudan exposure

## 實作時程與檢查點

**Immediate Effect**: Decision entered into force upon publication (29 January 2026)

**Member State Actions**:
- Implement asset freezes and travel bans immediately
- Update national enforcement databases
- Notify border authorities and financial institutions

**Ongoing Monitoring**:
- Track RSF procurement networks (Algoney Hamdan Dagalo Musa companies)
- Monitor SAF recruitment activities (Al-Tayyib Al-Imam Joda clan networks)
- Coordinate with UN sanctions committee for evidence sharing

## 風險與注意事項

**Atrocity Documentation**:
- **El Fasher atrocities** (October 2025): Civilian executions, killings of children, detention harassment
- **Khartoum North and Gezirah**: Summary executions by allied militia
- **Recruitment incitement**: Violence against civilian populations, support for paramilitaries

**Accountability Framework**:
- Listings reference specific locations and time periods (El Fasher October 2025)
- Documentation from UN Human Rights Council Fact-Finding Mission (December 2025)
- Corroboration from Human Rights Watch (February 2025)

**Compliance Challenges**:
- RSF procurement companies may operate through fronts or intermediaries
- Clan-based recruitment networks difficult to trace
- Cross-border financial flows require regional coordination

**Enforcement Priorities**:
- Arms procurement channels (Algoney Hamdan Dagalo Musa networks)
- Financial flows supporting RSF operations
- Recruitment funding for SAF-allied militias
- Cross-border asset holdings

## 與其他法規的關聯
