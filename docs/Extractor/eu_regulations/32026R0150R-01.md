**原始法規標題**: Amending Regulation (EU) 2021/1173 concerning the establishment of the Joint Undertaking for European High-Performance Computing

# L2 修正內容

## 修正範圍

修正 Regulation (EU) 2026/150 之 Articles 2 and 3。

## 錯誤內容

原文誤將以下內容拆分為兩個條文：
- Article 2: 聲稱 Regulation (EU) No 2018/1488 被廢止
- Article 3: 包含生效條款

## 正確內容

修正為單一 Article 2，內容為：
"This regulation shall enter into force on the day following publication in the Official Journal of the European Union."
