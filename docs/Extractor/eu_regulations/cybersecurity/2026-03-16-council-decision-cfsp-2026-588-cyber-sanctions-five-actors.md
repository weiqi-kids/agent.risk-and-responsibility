## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU member states, financial institutions, EU entities transacting with designated cyber threat actors, cybersecurity compliance officers
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: The CFSP sanctions framework for cyber-attacks is expanded by designating five additional actors (two Chinese individuals and three entities including an Iranian cyber actor) under Decision (CFSP) 2019/797.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Cybersecurity (state-sponsored APT activity, hacking-for-hire operations)
- Critical infrastructure (attacks on EU member state infrastructure by designated entities)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Travel ban for natural persons: Chen Cheng (COO, Anxun) and Wu Haibo (CEO, Anxun)
  - Asset freeze for all five designated parties: Chen Cheng, Wu Haibo, Anxun Information Technology Co. Ltd., Integrity Technology Group, Emennet Pasargad
  - Member states must implement travel bans and refuse entry
  - EU entities must not make funds or economic resources available to designated parties
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Anxun designated as "hacking-for-hire" company targeting EU member state critical infrastructure
  - Integrity Technology Group designated for facilitating APT Flax Typhoon attacks
  - Emennet Pasargad (Iranian) designated for targeting EU digital infrastructure and democratic processes
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
