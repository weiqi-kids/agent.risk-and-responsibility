## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU fishing vessel operators, Member State fisheries authorities, European Commission (DG MARE), Cook Islands Government fisheries department
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: 建立歐盟與庫克群島間新的漁業夥伴關係協議（2025-2032），授權歐盟船隻在庫克群島水域捕魚，並要求遵循永續漁業與勞動條件標準

## L3 — Risk Domains
- 供應鏈管理（漁業資源永續利用）
- 環境責任（西太平洋中部海洋資源保護）
- 勞動條件合規（漁業從業人員體面工作條件）
- 國際貿易協議執行

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - EU fishing vessels operating in Cook Islands waters must comply with the Protocol provisions (2025-2032)
  - Promote sustainable fisheries policies and responsible exploitation of fishery resources in the Western and Central Pacific Ocean
  - Support decent working conditions within the fishing industry
  - Implement budgetary commitments divisible into annual installments over the protocol period
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Fishing activity logs from Union vessels in Cook Islands waters
  - Compliance documentation for sustainable fisheries practices
  - Labor condition verification records for fishing industry workers
  - Annual budgetary execution reports
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
此決議批准歐盟與庫克群島簽署並暫時適用新漁業夥伴協議議定書，涵蓋 2025-2032 年期間。重點在於永續漁業管理與勞動條件保護。文件已於 2025 年 12 月 23 日在官方公報 L 系列公布，生效日期為 2025 年 11 月 12 日（決議通過日期）。此類國際協議 Decision 屬直接適用，無需成員國轉置立法。
