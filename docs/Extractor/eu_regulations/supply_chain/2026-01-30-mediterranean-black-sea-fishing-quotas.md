## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Union fishing vessels, member state fisheries authorities, fishery operators in Spain, France, Italy, Croatia, Greece, Slovenia, Bulgaria, Romania
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Establishes 2026 catch limits and fishing effort restrictions for Mediterranean and Black Sea waters covering 20+ fish stocks with specific closure periods, fleet capacity ceilings, and mandatory eel fishing bans

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Supply chain management
- Environmental compliance
- Resource sustainability

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Implement catch limits and fishing effort restrictions for demersal, pelagic, and deep-water shrimp stocks
  - Comply with fleet capacity ceilings for common dolphinfish and small pelagics in Adriatic
  - Observe turbot closure from 15 April to 15 June
  - Enforce minimum conservation reference size of 25 mm carapace length for Norway lobster
  - Record and transmit fishing effort data using specified codes to Commission
  - Enforce at least six-month commercial fishing closures for European eel
  - Ban recreational fishing for European eel
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Catch reporting and quota compliance documentation
  - Fishing effort data transmission records
  - Closure period enforcement records
  - Vessel capacity compliance documentation
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Applies 1 January 2026 through 31 December 2026. Covers approximately 20+ fish stocks across GFCM geographical subareas.
