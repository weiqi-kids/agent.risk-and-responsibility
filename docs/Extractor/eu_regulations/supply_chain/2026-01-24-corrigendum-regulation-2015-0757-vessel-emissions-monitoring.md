## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**:
  - Shipping companies
  - Maritime transport operators
  - Verifiers of emissions data
  - EU Member States port authorities
  - European Commission
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: 修正原 Regulation (EU) 2015/757 Article 4(6) 用語，將資料完整性保證標準從「reasonable certainty」改為「appropriate assurance」，澄清船舶 CO2 排放監測與報告的資料完整性要求

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Maritime emissions monitoring
- Climate compliance
- Supply chain transparency
- Data integrity assurance

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Companies must ensure CO2 emissions data integrity can be determined with appropriate assurance (revised standard from reasonable certainty)
  - Implement data integrity controls meeting the revised appropriate assurance standard for emissions monitoring and reporting systems
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated MRV (Monitoring, Reporting, Verification) documentation reflecting corrected data integrity requirements
  - Emissions monitoring systems with appropriate assurance controls
  - Verification records demonstrating compliance with revised Article 4(6)
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
此為 Regulation (EU) 2015/757 的第 2 次勘誤（R(02)），修正 Article 4(6) 關於 CO2 排放資料完整性保證標準的用語。原規範為船舶 CO2 排放監測、報告與驗證（MRV）系統，與 EU ETS 海運擴展相關。勘誤將資料完整性要求從「reasonable certainty」修正為「appropriate assurance」，影響所有受監管的海運公司及其資料驗證程序。
