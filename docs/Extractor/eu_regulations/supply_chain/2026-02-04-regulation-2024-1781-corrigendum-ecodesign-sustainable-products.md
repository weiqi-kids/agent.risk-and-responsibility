## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Product manufacturers, importers, distributors, authorized representatives, Member State market surveillance authorities, European Commission
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Technical corrections to ecodesign regulation clarifying definitions, delegated act provisions, substances of concern terminology, digital product passport requirements, and labeling display requirements

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Supply chain responsibility (environmental sustainability)
- Product compliance and certification
- Digital market (product information disclosure)
- Data protection (digital product passports)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Corrected language regarding animal feed definitions and consumer definitions
  - Clarified delegated act provisions for ecodesign requirements
  - Revised substances of concern terminology
  - Updated digital product passport requirements language
  - Corrected labeling display requirements for ecodesign information
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Digital product passport documentation
  - Ecodesign compliance records
  - Labeling and product information materials
  - Substances of concern declarations
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
The corrigendum does not concern the English version - corrections apply to Czech language provisions. As a corrigendum to an EU Regulation, these corrections have direct binding effect across all Member States without need for transposition. The original regulation creates a comprehensive framework for sustainable product design affecting multiple product categories.
