## L4: 操作衝擊

### new_obligations

**For EU Member States**:
- Cede exclusive competence to Union during agreement operational period
- Implement agreement terms through domestic transport regulations
- Coordinate with Ukrainian authorities on road freight operations
- Resume full competence upon agreement termination

**For EU and Ukrainian Road Haulage Operators**:
- Comply with amended agreement terms (specific provisions in amending agreement)
- Adhere to permit requirements and operational conditions
- Meet safety and technical standards specified in agreement

**For Joint Committee**:
- Periodic review of agreement implementation (referenced meeting: 18 December 2023)
- Monitor operational effectiveness
- Recommend adjustments as needed

### evidence_requirements

**Documentation Required**:
- Original amending agreement signature documentation (20 June 2024)
- Notification of consent to be bound (Article 3 obligation)
- Joint Committee meeting records and review reports
- Operational compliance records from road haulage operators

**Monitoring Evidence**:
- Transport permit issuance records
- Cross-border freight volume data
- Compliance inspection reports

### enforcement_signal

**Enforcement Mechanism**: Joint Committee oversight with periodic review capacity.

**Enforcement Authority**:
- Joint Committee (EU-Ukraine)
- National transport authorities in Member States and Ukraine
- European Commission (coordination)

**Temporal Limitation**: Agreement extended to 30 June 2025 with tacit renewal possibility for six-month periods, ensuring competence doesn't permanently transfer.

**Compliance Timeline**: Immediate (provisionally applied since 20 June 2024)

**Union Obligations**:
- Notify consent to be bound (Article 3)
- Cease competence exercise upon agreement termination
- Restore Member State competence without prejudice to other Union measures

## Notes

**Purpose**: Maintains EU-Ukraine road freight transport cooperation during Russia's ongoing invasion of Ukraine, facilitating trade and supply chain continuity.

**Legal Basis**: Treaty on the Functioning of the European Union provisions on transport policy and external relations

**Original Agreement Date**: 29 June 2022 (established during Ukraine conflict)

**Amendment Signature**: 20 June 2024

**Provisional Application**: Since 20 June 2024

**Competence Framework**: Shared competence temporarily transferred to Union, reverting to Member States upon agreement termination.

**Context**: Part of EU's broader support for Ukraine's economic stability and trade connectivity during ongoing conflict with Russia. Agreement ensures continued road freight operations between EU and Ukraine despite war-related disruptions.

**Review Mechanism**: Joint Committee meeting (18 December 2023) demonstrates ongoing oversight and adjustment capacity.
