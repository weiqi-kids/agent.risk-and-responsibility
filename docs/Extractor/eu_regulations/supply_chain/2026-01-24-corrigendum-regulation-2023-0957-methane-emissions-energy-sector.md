## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**:
  - Maritime transport operators
  - Shipping companies
  - Emissions verifiers (third-party auditors)
  - EU Member States maritime authorities
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: 技術性勘誤，修正 Regulation (EU) 2023/957 第 1 條第 14(b) 點用語，將驗證人員評估標準從「adequate certainty」改為「adequate assurance」，澄清海運溫室氣體排放數據驗證程序的專業術語

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Maritime emissions verification quality
- Climate reporting accuracy
- Third-party auditor standards

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Verifiers must assess company-level aggregate emissions data compliance using the standard of "adequate assurance" (not "adequate certainty")
  - Verifiers must determine whether aggregate emissions data contains materially misleading information
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Verification reports using corrected terminology
  - Assessment documentation demonstrating "adequate assurance" standard applied
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
此勘誤僅修正單一語言表述（adequate certainty → adequate assurance），不涉及實質義務變更。原 Regulation (EU) 2023/957 將海運活動納入 EU 排放交易系統（ETS），並要求監測、報告與驗證額外溫室氣體及船舶類型的排放。此勘誤影響驗證人員執行評估時的專業標準表述，屬於技術性修正。
