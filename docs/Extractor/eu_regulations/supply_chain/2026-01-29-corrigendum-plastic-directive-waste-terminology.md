## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Member states, producers of single-use plastic products, fishing gear manufacturers, waste management authorities
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Clarifies that producer responsibility applies specifically to "abandoned waste" rather than all "waste" to better define environmental impact obligations

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Environmental responsibility
- Supply chain transparency
- Producer extended responsibility (EPR) compliance

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - No new substantive obligations (corrigendum only clarifies existing terminology)
  - Original directive obligations remain: cleanup costs for abandoned waste, marketing restrictions, consumer information provision
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Waste generation calculations for plastic products placed on market
  - Documentation of cleanup cost coverage for abandoned plastic waste
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
Corrigendum does not affect English version. The terminology clarification from "waste" to "abandoned waste" helps member states better target enforcement of producer responsibility schemes for environmental cleanup costs.
