## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU agricultural producers and industry associations, Mercosur exporting countries (Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, Uruguay), EU Member States, EU outermost regions, European Commission
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: Establishes a new regulatory framework implementing bilateral safeguard mechanisms under EU-Mercosur trade agreements, allowing temporary tariff preference withdrawals for sensitive agricultural products during import surges to protect EU producers.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Agricultural supply chain protection from import surges
- EU-Mercosur trade agreement implementation
- Trade safeguard and enforcement mechanisms

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Commission must continuously monitor sensitive agricultural products and report every six months
  - Investigations into import injury must conclude within 4–6 months
  - Provisional safeguard measures limited to maximum 200 calendar days
  - Definitive measures cannot exceed four years total duration
  - Safeguard measures restricted to transition period (12–18 years from agreement entry into force)
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Annual reporting to European Parliament and Council required
  - Commission adopts implementing acts through specified committee procedures
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
