## L1 — Rule Signal

- **document_type**: Directive
- **binding_force**: requires_transposition

- **rule_type**: Corrigendum to Directive
- **doc_id**: 32019L0883R(01)
- **published_date**: 2025-12-19
- **effective_date**: Follows parent Directive (EU) 2019/883 implementation timeline
- **parent_directive**: Directive (EU) 2019/883 on port reception facilities for ship waste
- **amends**: Annex 2 and Annex 3 classification errors in original directive
- **repeals**: N/A (corrigendum only corrects existing text)
- **scope**: Technical correction to cargo residue waste classification under MARPOL Annex V

## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **affected_roles**: Port operators, port reception facility managers, shipping companies, vessel masters, maritime waste management authorities, EU member states
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Corrects mislabeling of HME (high-viscosity) versus non-HME cargo residue types J and K in waste classification tables, ensuring accurate waste handling procedures at port reception facilities

## L3 — Risk Domains

- **primary_domain**: Supply chain compliance (maritime waste management)
- **secondary_domains**: Environmental protection, maritime operations
- **risk_shift**: Eliminates confusion in waste categorization that could lead to improper handling of ship-generated cargo residues
- **interconnection**: Links to MARPOL Convention Annex V (garbage from ships), Directive 2010/65/EU (reporting formalities), and repealed Directive 2000/59/EC

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence

- **new_obligations**: No new substantive obligations; corrects technical classification errors in Annex 2 Point 3 and Annex 3 Point 3 swapping HME/non-HME designations for cargo residue categories J and K
- **evidence_requirements**: Port reception facilities must apply corrected waste classifications when receiving and processing ship waste deliveries
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes

- **context**: This is a corrigendum (correction notice) published in Official Journal L 2025/91039, fixing administrative errors in the original 2019 directive without altering substantive policy
- **classification_correction**: Swaps high-viscosity (HME) and non-HME labels between cargo residue types J and K in both Annex 2 and Annex 3
- **no_new_deadlines**: Does not introduce new compliance timelines; corrections integrate into existing directive implementation
- **marpol_linkage**: Corrections ensure alignment with MARPOL Annex V garbage classification framework
