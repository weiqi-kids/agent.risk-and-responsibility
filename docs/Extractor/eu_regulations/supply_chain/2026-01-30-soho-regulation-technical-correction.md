## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: SoHO establishments (substances of human origin), competent authorities, EU SoHO Platform operators, healthcare providers across Member States
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Clarifies authorization requirements for SoHO establishments and triggers for rapid alert activation on EU SoHO Platform

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Supply chain (human-origin substances traceability and safety)
- Critical infrastructure (healthcare system integrity)

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Article 24(4) correction: Entities that do NOT enter the definition of SoHO establishment require authorization (clarifying scope)
  - Article 34(1) correction: Rapid alert on EU SoHO Platform triggered when quality/safety/supply implications affect MORE THAN ONE Member State (not just one)
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Authorization documentation for non-SoHO establishments handling human substances
  - Cross-border incident reporting when multiple Member States are affected
  - Platform alert records demonstrating multi-state impact threshold
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
此更正文件修正了兩處實質性錯誤：(1) Article 24(4) 從「符合定義」改為「不符合定義」，影響授權範圍判定；(2) Article 34(1) 從「一個或多個成員國」改為「多於一個成員國」，提高快速警報啟動門檻。本規則取代 Directives 2002/98/CE 和 2004/23/CE。此更正不影響英文版（僅修正其他語言版本）。
