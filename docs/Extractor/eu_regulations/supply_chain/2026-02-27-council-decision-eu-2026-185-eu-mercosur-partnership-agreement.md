## Notes
WebFetch successful. This decision authorises signing of the long-negotiated EU-Mercosur Association Agreement's trade pillar and enables provisional application of specified chapters. Full ratification requires EU Parliament consent and national ratification. The Agreement covers the EU (and its Member States) and four Mercosur countries: Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, and Uruguay. Businesses with EU-South America supply chains should monitor the Official Journal for the provisional application start date and assess compliance requirements under the new framework.
