## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **affected_roles**: EU Intellectual Property Office (EUIPO), Member State Courts, Design Rights Holders, Design Applicants, Legal Practitioners in IP Law
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: This corrigendum clarifies terminology and article titles in Regulation (EU) 2024/2822 to ensure consistency in Community design registration procedures, judicial measures, and rights holder documentation across all Member States.

## L3 — Risk Domains

**Affected Areas:**
- **Intellectual Property Registration** — 設計註冊的形式要件、保護期限計算
- **Digital Market Compliance** — 數位設計註冊及檔案查閱程序
- **Judicial Procedures** — 臨時措施、管轄權術語的精準化

**Risk Exposure:**
- 中度 — 術語修正影響已進行中的設計註冊案件解釋
- 低度 — 實質義務未變動,僅為文字一致性調整

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence

- **new_obligations**: Use corrected terminology in all design registration procedures and court documents effective 2026-01-20; apply "companies" instead of "commercial societies", "change of rights holder" instead of "modification in property right", "conditions of form" instead of "formal requirements", "provisional measures" instead of "conservatory measures"; ensure article titles reflect corrected terminology including "Commencement and duration" for protection periods.
- **evidence_requirements**: Official corrigendum text published in the Official Journal of the EU (OJ); updated procedural documents from EUIPO using corrected terminology; court judgments and legal filings applying the revised terms for provisional measures and jurisdiction; registration certificates issued after 2026-01-20 reflecting the corrected terminology.
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes

**分類理由:**
此文件為共同體設計制度的勘誤,涉及數位時代的設計註冊及市場保護機制,歸類為 digital_market。雖涉及知識產權,但核心影響在於數位設計註冊系統的運作。

**Corrigendum 性質:**
此為技術性勘誤,修正 12 處術語及條文標題,不改變實質義務內容。主要目的為確保法規文字的專業一致性及跨語言版本的精準性。

**影響範圍:**
- 直接適用於所有 27 個成員國
- 影響 EUIPO 及成員國法院的程序文件用語
- 不具追溯效力,僅適用於勘誤生效後的新案件
