## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: EU member states, financial institutions, technology companies providing services in EU, border control authorities, asset management entities
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Expands sanctions list by adding 15 individuals and 6 entities involved in human rights violations, internet censorship, and protest suppression in Iran

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Digital rights and censorship
- Human rights compliance
- Cross-border sanctions enforcement

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Implement asset freezes against 15 newly listed Iranian officials
  - Enforce travel bans on listed individuals
  - Apply restrictions to 6 entities including cyberspace regulatory bodies and software companies
  - Prohibit making funds or economic resources available to listed persons/entities
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Due diligence checks against updated sanctions list
  - Transaction monitoring for listed entities (SATRA, Seraj Cyberspace Organization, Yaftar Pazhohan, Douran Software Technologies, Masaf Institute, Working Group for Determining Instances of Criminal Content)
  - Border control verification for travel restrictions
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This decision specifically targets entities involved in internet censorship and disinformation, including Iran's Audio-Visual Media Regulatory Authority (SATRA) and software companies providing surveillance technology. Technology companies operating in EU must ensure compliance with restrictions on providing services to these listed entities.
