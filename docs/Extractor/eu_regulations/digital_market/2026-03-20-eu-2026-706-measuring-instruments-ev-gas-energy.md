## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Manufacturers of measuring instruments, distributors, conformity assessment bodies, Member State regulatory authorities
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: Expands the scope of Directive 2014/32/EU to cover measuring systems for electric vehicle charging equipment, compressed gas dispensers for hydrogen and alternative fuels, thermal energy meters for cooling, and smart meters supporting DC and alternative fuel gases.

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Digital market regulation and measuring instrument standards
- Electric vehicle infrastructure compliance
- Alternative fuel and energy metering

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Manufacturers must comply with updated essential requirements in amended Annexes I, IV, V, and new Annexes Va and VIIa
  - Member States must ensure conformity assessment bodies are notified and authorized before implementation date
  - No retrofitting mandates for existing charging infrastructure
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Conformity assessment certification required for all covered measuring instruments
  - Certificates issued under prior law remain valid through 10 April 2038
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory
