## L1 — Rule Signal

- **document_type**: Decision
- **binding_force**: directly_applicable

- **rule_type**: new
- **rule_id**: CELEX:32026D0125
- **jurisdiction**: EU + Singapore
- **effective_date**: 2025-12-08

Council Decision (EU) 2026/125 of 8 December 2025 approving the conclusion of the Agreement on Digital Trade between the European Union and the Republic of Singapore. This decision establishes the legal basis for EU participation in bilateral digital trade commitments with Singapore.

## L2 — Responsibility Structure

- **affected_roles**: EU Member States, EU institutions involved in digital trade policy implementation, economic operators engaging in EU-Singapore digital trade
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: 建立歐盟與新加坡數位貿易協定的法律基礎，影響跨境數位服務提供者與電子商務營運商的合規框架

## L3 — Risk Domains

- **primary**: Digital Market (數位市場規範)
- **secondary**: Data Protection (資料保護，因 EDPS 已諮詢), Cross-border Trade Compliance
- **intersection**: 數位貿易協定可能與 GDPR、DSA、DMA 等現有 EU 數位法規產生適用優先順序或豁免條款的交互作用

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence

- **new_obligations**: EU 及成員國須遵守 EU-Singapore Digital Trade Agreement 實質條款（詳見關聯文件 22026A00126），包含跨境資料流動、電子簽章互認、數位產品市場准入等義務
- **evidence_requirements**: 協定實施後，相關經濟營運商可能需證明符合雙邊數位貿易協定條款，包括來源地證明、數位服務合規聲明等
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes

此 Decision 本身為批准決議，實質義務內容載於另一關聯文件（Agreement on Digital Trade, document 22026A00126）。European Data Protection Supervisor (EDPS) 已就資料保護影響進行諮詢。生效日期為通過日 2025-12-08，無轉置期限（Decision 直接生效）。
