## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: contracting authorities, selected bidders, procurement officers, member state transposition bodies
- **shift_type**: clarified
- **shift_summary**: Corrects article cross-references in Directive 2014/24/EU Article 59(4) to clarify that contracting authorities must reference Articles 60 and 62 (not 59 and 60) when requesting updated documentary evidence from selected bidders before contract award

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Public procurement procedures

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**:
  - Contracting authorities must apply corrected article references (Articles 60 and 62) when requesting updated documentary evidence
- **evidence_requirements**:
  - Updated documentary evidence from selected bidders before contract award (per corrected articles)
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
This is the 19th corrigendum to Directive 2014/24/EU. The correction does not concern the English version. The corrigendum specifically addresses Article 59, paragraph 4, second subparagraph, first sentence, correcting internal cross-references to ensure proper procedural guidance for contracting authorities.
