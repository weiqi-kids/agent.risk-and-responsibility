## Notes
Corrigendum does not affect English version. This is the 12th corrigendum to the eIDAS regulation, addressing only Maltese language grammatical consistency for terminology related to electronic delivery services. No substantive legal obligations are changed. The correction ensures proper singular/plural forms and qualified/unqualified service designations in Maltese text.
