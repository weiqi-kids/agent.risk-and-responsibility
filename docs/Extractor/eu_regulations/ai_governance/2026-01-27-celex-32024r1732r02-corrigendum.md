## Notes

This corrigendum corrects terminology in the Estonian language version of Council Regulation (EU) 2024/1732, which strengthens Europe's role in trustworthy AI through the EuroHPC Initiative for startups. The change from "factory" to "facility" affects all grammatical cases throughout the regulation but does not alter substantive rights, obligations, or compliance mechanisms. The original regulation (2024/1732) amended Regulation (EU) 2021/1173 concerning the European High Performance Computing Joint Undertaking.
