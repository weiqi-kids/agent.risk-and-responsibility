## L1 — Rule Signal
- **rule_type**: draft
- **issuing_body**: NIST
- **document_id**: NISTIR 8596
- **status**: public_comment

## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: Organizations at any stage of AI adoption, cybersecurity professionals, enterprise leadership, AI integration decision-makers, all sectors (manufacturing, financial, telecommunications, etc.)
- **shift_type**: new
- **shift_summary**: 建立首個 AI 網路安全框架配置檔,要求組織在採用 AI 時必須同步發展網路安全策略,涵蓋保護 AI 系統、使用 AI 強化防禦及抵禦 AI 驅動攻擊三大領域

## L3 — Risk Domains
- AI system security within organizational infrastructure
- AI-enabled cyberattack resilience
- Rapid AI advancement and emerging risks
- AI integration across operational areas
- Defensive cybersecurity operations enhancement
- Strategic AI adoption risk management

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**: Strategically adopt AI while addressing emerging cybersecurity risks; develop cybersecurity strategies acknowledging AI advancement across all operations; secure AI systems within infrastructure; use AI to enhance defensive cybersecurity operations; build resilience against AI-enabled cyberattacks
- **evidence_requirements**: AI cybersecurity strategy documentation; AI system security assessments; defensive AI capability implementations; AI-enabled threat resilience measures; cross-operational AI risk assessments
- **enforcement_signal**: recommended

## Notes
Preliminary draft released December 16, 2025. Public feedback solicited through January 30, 2026. Developed with over 6,500 community members through yearlong stakeholder engagement. Joins existing CSF profiles for manufacturing, financial, and telecommunications sectors.
