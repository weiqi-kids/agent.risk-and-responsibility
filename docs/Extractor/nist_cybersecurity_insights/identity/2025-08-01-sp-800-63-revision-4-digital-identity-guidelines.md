## L1 — Rule Signal
- **rule_type**: final
- **issuing_body**: NIST Identity and Access Management Program
- **document_id**: NIST SP 800-63 Revision 4, Digital Identity Guidelines
- **status**: final

## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: federal agencies, identity service providers, credential service providers, authentication system developers, fraud prevention teams
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: SP 800-63 Revision 4 擴大身份風險管理範圍，重構風險管理流程為跨職能協作，新增對抗注入攻擊與深偽的控制措施，整合 synced passkeys，更新密碼組成與輪替期望，並納入訂閱者控制錢包於聯合模型

## L3 — Risk Domains
- Digital identity risk management
- Authentication security
- Identity proofing integrity
- Fraud prevention
- Federated identity trust

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**: Implement controls against injection attacks and deepfake media in identity proofing; update password composition and rotation policies to align with Revision 4 guidance; consider continuous assessment metrics for identity assurance
- **evidence_requirements**: Documentation of identity proofing controls, authentication method implementation records, fraud prevention measures
- **enforcement_signal**: mandatory

## Notes
歷時近四年開發，經過兩次公開草案與約 6,000 條公眾意見，正式於 2025-08-01 發布。與 2017 年版本相比，Revision 4 主要變更包含：風險管理框架重構、身份驗證控制架構重整、syncable authenticators 整合、密碼政策更新、聯合身份模型擴充。WebFetch 已使用。
