## L1 — Rule Signal
- **rule_type**: draft
- **issuing_body**: NIST IoT Cybersecurity Program
- **document_id**: NIST IR 8259 Revision 1 (Second Public Draft)
- **status**: public_comment

## L2 — Responsibility Structure
- **affected_roles**: IoT device manufacturers, product security teams, supply chain managers, customers of IoT products
- **shift_type**: expanded
- **shift_summary**: NIST IR 8259 修訂版擴大 pre-market 與 post-market 活動範圍，新增 Activity 0（整合威脅建模與初始風險評估），原 Activity 3 拆分為 Activities 3 和 4，強化產品網路安全能力的識別與實作指引

## L3 — Risk Domains
- IoT product cybersecurity
- Threat modeling
- Risk assessment
- Manufacturer accountability
- Customer cybersecurity expectations

## L4 — Obligation & Evidence
- **new_obligations**: Manufacturers should integrate threat modeling and initial risk assessment into pre-market activities (Activity 0), clearly document user operational responsibilities, establish governance and accountability mechanisms
- **evidence_requirements**: Documentation of product cybersecurity capabilities aligned with customer needs, transparent disclosure of user responsibilities, evidence of threat modeling and risk assessment
- **enforcement_signal**: recommended
